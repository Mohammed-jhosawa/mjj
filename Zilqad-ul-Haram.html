09- Urus: Syedna Feerkhan Shajauddin R.A (Ahmedabad)

9 Zilqad-ul-Haram - Syedna Feerkhan Shajauddin (r.a.) (Ahmedabad)

33ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Feerkhan Shajauddin (r.a.)
bin Ahmed bin Malekshah,
Vafat: 9 Zilqad-ul-Haram 1065 H., 1655 A.D Ahmedabad

• Fitrat na zaman ma Deen ni hifazat na khatir Syedna Feerkhan Shajauddin (r.a.) qayam thaya.

• Aap hamesha ilm hasil karva ma ijtehad ane saaye-baleeg karta rahya. Apna ayyam ( Period ) musibat na ayyam tha, te saath bhi aap ye ilm nashar kari ne azeem olamaa (Scholars) taiyar kida.

• Aap Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed (r.a.) na saath Qaidkhana (Jail) ma hata. Aap par je zulm thaya chhe, tehni zikar Syedi Hasanji Badshah (a.q.) ye "Kitabul Tazkarah" ma kidi chhe.

• Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed (r.a.) ni shahadat na baad mumineen par ghano zulm thayo.

Aurangzeb Syedna Shajauddin (r.a.) ne qaidi na haal ma Lahore lai gayo. Vahan aap ne ghora na ek astabal ma rakhela. Vahan je mojezo thayo te mashhur chhe. Astabal ma mohti aag laagi. Aag (Fire) ma saghla astabal jali gaya, magar aap no muqam jahan hato te jagah salamat rahi. Aa mojeza na sabab Mughal Badshah Shahjahan ye aap ne reha kari, maan ikram na saath Ahmedabad vapas mokla. Aap 1058 H. ma Ahmedabad vapas pohncha.

• Aap na Dushmano ye yahan bhi aap ne sataya. Gujarat na Suba Gairatkhan ne shikayat kidi, aap ne fari qaid karaya. Aap 18 mahina qaid ma rahya. Baad ma aap ni rehai thai.

• Aap na zaman ma Hujumiyah firqa ni fitnat thai. Dawat nu den [debt] ghanu thai gayu, je Syedna Ismail Badruddin Saheb (r.a.) ye adaa kidu.

• 4 Zilqad 1065 H. aap ye Moulai Hasanfir Saheb ni ziyarat vaste irado kido. Safar shuru kidi. Rasta ma ek talab (Pond) na najdeek namaz parhi. Namaz na baad aap na pet ma ghanu dard ( ain) shuru thayu. Aap ye kalam na kida. Hatta ke aap 9 Zilqad wafat thaya.

• Aap ni qabr mubarak Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed (r.a.) na jivaar ma chhe.

• Syedna Shujauddin (r.a.) na baad Dawat nu markaz Ahmedabad si Jamnagar muntaqil (Transfer) thayu.

11- Urus: Syedi Hasan bin Nooh, Bharuchi Badruddin AQ (Yemen)

11 Zilqadul Haram - Syedi Hasan bin Nooh, Bharuchi Badruddin a.q (Yemen)

Almaulal Faazil,
Syedi Hasan bin Nooh, Bharuchi Badruddin a.q
bin Yusuf bin Mohammed bin Adam
Vafat: 11 Zilqadul Haram, 945 H.[ 1538 A.D.]- Masaar, Yemen
Zaman: 23ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Mohammed bin Syedna Hasan r.a.

• Aap Bharuch na rehvasi hata. Hindustan si 873H [1469 A.D.] ma 20 varas ni umare aap Yemen aaya. Aap Maletujjar vepario na badahah ginata. Aap na bava-Dada Gujarat na aagevan vepario hata. Aap no vepar Hindustan, Yemen, Mecca, Misr ane Shaam na shehro ma felaelo hato.

• Aap Masaar ma rehta hata. 20ma Dail Ajal, Syedna Hasan Badruddin (r.a.) ni hazrat ma rahi ne aap ye ilm hasil kidu, yahan tak ke aap ye ek azimus-shan kitab likha, je nu naam " Kitabul Azhaar" chhe.

• Doat Kiram ye aap ne Hind, Sind ane Yemen na jazira ma ilm na nizam ni ane ilm nashar karvani raza farmavi, ane farmayu ke aap jene chaho, ane je shaan si chaho, ilm parhavo. Aap ye aa raza maubarak ni barakat si Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin (r.a.), Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin (r.a.) ane Moulai Malak bin Firoz ilm nasar kidu.

• Dawate Hadiyah na kuul [total] umur chare Doat na zaman ma aap ni taraf rahya.

Moulai Raj bin Moulai Hasan, Maulai Abdul Wahab bin Maulai Firoz, Maulai Qasamji, aa saghla Vulatul Hind, kuul umur aap ni taraf pesh karta, ane Vajebat arz karta.

• Aap ye Syedna Hasan Badruddin (r.a.) si Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin (r.a.) tak chaar (4) Doat ni khidmat kidi. Aap jano maal si Dawat par nisar tha. Dushmano si jehad ma hazaro rupiya kharachnar tha. Khuda Taala ye aap ni khidmat ni jazaa ye aapi ke aap ni nasal ma Haq na Dai Syedna Ali Shamshuddin (r.a.)-30ma Dai- thaya.

• Aap na Shehzadi Naamatbai ni shaadi Syedi Idris bin Syedna Ali Shamshuddin [22ma dai] (a.q.) na saath thai. Aap na shikam si Syedi Hasan (a.q.) janaya, ane ehna farzand Dail Ajal Syedna Ali Shamshuddin (r.a.) [30ma Dai] chhe.

• Aap 24ma Dai, Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin (r.a.) na zaman ma 11 Zilqadul Haram 945 H.

ma wafat thaya. Aap ni qabr mubarak Masaar na killa na darvaza na qareeb chhe.

• Aap ni umar 95 varas ni hati.

• Aap nu laqab Badruddin chhe.

• Taybah-Yemen ma aap ye ek Masjid banavi chhe.

• Aap na dikri Fatema ni qabr Taybah (Yemen) ma chhe.magar maaruf nathi.

12- Urus: Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin AQ [43rd Dai]

12 Zilqadul Haram - Urus

35ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Abdultaiyeb Zakiuddin (r.a.)
najle Dail Ajal Syedna Ismail Badruddin (r.a.)
Vafat: 12 Zilqadul Haram 1110 H. 1699 A.D. Jamnagar

• 34ma Dai, Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a. Saheb ye Syedna Abdultaiyeb Zakiuddin (r.a.

) par nas farmavi, te waqt aap ni umar 28 varas ni hati. Aap ne Mazoon na rutba ma qayam kida. Aap Hafezul Quran tha.

• Aap ye aap na bavaji saheb ni azeem khidmat kidi, ane har umur ma aap na moaavin rahya.

• Aap ni koshisho na sabab Hujumia firqa na logo tauba kari ne Dawat ma dakhal thaya.

• Syedna Badruddin (r.a.) na wafat na baad aap Ahmedabad padhara. Dawat na elaam ne buland kida. Mukhalefeen ne hasad aayo, fitnat ni aag salghavi, aap ne ane Mumineen ne ghani musibat pohnchi. Ghana bilad ma gharo lutai gaya, Mumineen ne qaid kida, ane ghana ne qatal kida. Ahmdabad na beve maqbara ma Hudud akrameen ni quboor mitavi didi.

Doat na Qubbat todi nakha. Masjido veran thai gai.

• Syedna Zakiuddin (r.a.) makhfi ma Jamnagar padhari gaya. Khuda Taala ye aap ni hifazat kidi. Jamnagar na raja "Jam Tamanchi" ye aap ni ghani khidmat kidi.

• Aap ye Jamnagar ma kuvo khodayo, ehnu naam 'Beer-e-Ali" chhe.

• 72 Varas ni umare aap 12 Zilqadul Haram 1110 H. vafat thaya. Aap ni Qabr Mubarak Jamnagar ma Qubbat Badriyah ma chhe.

• Aap na Aqeela (wife) Ummul Mumineen Fatema Aaisaheba binte Masoodkhan ni qabr aaj Qubbat ma chhe, je Syedna Moosa Kalimuddin (r.a.) ane Syedi Shekhadam Safiuddin (a.q.) na valeda (mother) chhe.

43ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a.
najle Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a.
Vafat: 12 Zilqad 1232 H. [1817 A.D.], Surat

• Aap Aale bharmal na nabira che, 24 varas ni naani umare Dai thaya..

• Dawat ni taarikh ma aap Moayyed-e-Asgar si olkhay chhe. Aap ye Dawat ma ilm ni naher jaari kidi.

• Dawat na arsh par aava baad aap thoro waqt Surat rahya, ne te baad Morbi hijrat kidi. Morbi ne aap ye "Darul Hijrat" banayu. Jamnagar ma aap ye Haveli ane Masjid ni tameer kidi.

• Mulk ma te arsaa ma ghani bad-amani [ disturbances] phelai. Mumineen vaste mehnat waqe thai. Loot ane zulm ni aafat aavi. Mumineen ni halaat najuk thai. Aap ye maal kharch kari saghla ne himmat aapi.

• 1214 H. ma Chandji nam na shakhs ye Aurangabad ma fisad felavi. Morbi ma te pakrayo, ane halak thayo. Aap te baad Surat padhara.

• 1220 H. ma aap ye 'Masjid-e-Azam' ane 'Saifee Daras' banaya.

• 1227 H. ma Cutch ma mohto dukal [Great Famine] paro. Aap ye 12000 Mumineen ne Surat bolavi aasro aapo, Anaj pohnchayu, Chrkha kantva nu farmayu.

• Ghana Mumineen ne aap ye Africa, Ceylon ane Burma vepar vaste java ni salah aapi.

Rangoon ma Masjid banavi. Karachi [Sind] ma Mumineen vasta thaya. Qareeb 270 Gaam ane 700 Masjid Sind ma bani. Africa ma Masjid bani. Qarib 25000 Mumineen wahan vasa.

• Syedna Taher Saifuddin [r.a.] ye aap ni chaar shaan ni zikar farmavi chhe:

1- Ilm ni naher nashar karva 'Saifee Daras' ni taameer.

2- Fatemi Falsafat ne zindaa kidi.

3- 'Siyasat-Rabbaniya' par Dawat na nizam ne abaad kidu.

4- Imam [a.s.] na fuyuz aap ni turbat par utra.

• 40 varas ni umare 1232 H. ma Surat ma aap vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr Mubarak Qubbat Najmiyah ma chhe. Aa varas 1432 ma aap na vafat ne pura 200 varas thaya.

13- Urus: Syedna Ali bin Syedna Husain [9th Dai]

13 Ziqadul Haram - 9ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Ali r.a. (Yemen)

9ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Ali r.a.
najle Syedna Husain r.a.
Vafat: 13 Ziqadul Haram, 682 H. 1284 A.D. Sana'a, Yemen

Mazoon-e-Dawat
Syedi Husain bin Syedna Ali Hanzala
Syedna Ali bin Syedna Husain r.a.
Mukasir-e-Dawat
Shk. Asad bin Hatim Sanjani

• 9ma Dai Syedna Ali r.a. najle Syedna Husain r.a. Aale Valid na chotha [4th] Dai chhe.

• Aap Sanaa ma muqim rahya. Sana'a ma fasad hato, tesi Sultan Mohammed bin Hatim ni arz par si aap Urus padhari gaya. Vahan aap ye shariat na maalim qayam kida. Mumineen ni susti duur kari. Quran Majid ni tilavat raat din, aap nu mohtu shugal hatu.

• Aap na Mazoon Syedi Husain bin Syedna Ali bin Hanzala r.a. tha. Aap Sanaa ma rehta. Mazoon na bhaio bhi khidmat ma saath hata. 677H. ma Mazoon Syedi Husain A.Q vafat thaya. Aap ne ghano sadmo pohncho. Mazoon na Matam ma aap ye Marasiyah likha chhe. Aap ye Syedna Ali bin Syedna Husain r.a. ne mazoon na rutba ma qayam kida.

• Aap na Mukasir Sheikhul Afzal Asad bin Hatim Sanjani bhi Sana'a ma hata.

• Sanaa ma fitnat na vadal duur thaata, Syedna Ali ye Sanaa ne pachhu Darul Hijrat banayu.

• Aap ye aap na Mazoon na dikra Syedna Ali bin Husain r.a. par nas farmavi.

• 13 Zilqadul Haram aap Sana'a ma vafat thaya. Aap ni Qabr mubarak Sana'a ma chhe, magar jagah maruf nathi.

15- Urus: Syedna Tayyib Zainuddin bin Syedi Jewanji [45th Dai]

15 Zilqadul Haram - Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin [r.a.] (Surat)

45ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin [r.a.]
bin Syedi Jivanji a.q.
Vafat: 15 Zilqadul Haram 1252 H. 1837 A.D., Surat


• Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin [r.a.] Syedi Jivanji a.q.[Burhanpur] na farzande anjuman chhe.

• Aap no zaman saadat [peace] no zaman hato. Aap noor ni misal tha. Dataar (Genorous) tha. Tolaba (Students) par ghani shafaqat rakhta. Gunegaro ne bakhshi deta.

Aap ye Taalim ane Moallim na qavaneen [rules] banaya.

• Angrez Sarkar ane Deshi Rajwada, beve ma aap nu ghanu maan (respect) hatu. Sir John Malcom, Holkar, Sindia saghla aap no maan martabo rakhta, aap ne ghani karamat (Respect) aapta.

• 1237 H. [ 1822 ] ma Surat ma ghani mohti rel [flood] aavi. Shaher (City) panch (5) din paani ma rahyu. Aap ye Mumineen ane gair Mumineen vaste naav (boats) firavi, anaaj ni madad pohnchavi.

• Aap ye aap na shahzada Syedna Abdul Qadar Najmuddin [r.a.] ane Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin [r.a.] ni Shaadi ghani dhamdhoom si Surat ma kidi, ane aap ni dukhtar (Daughter) ni Shaadi Syedna Mohammed Badruddin [r.a.] na saath kidi.

• Aap Bombay Legislative Council na member hata. Bombay Presidency taraf si aap ne Upper Council ni membership offer thai, aap ye inkar kido.

• Aap ye ghana Mashahed ane Madaris aabad kida. Syedi Fakhruddin Shaheed a.q. [Galiakot] na Roza,, Hasanfir Saheb ni Masjid, Surat na qubba ane Yemen ma Syedna Hatim r.a. na qubba aap ye banaya. Syedna Hatim r.a. na qubba aa qabal [pehla] jivare bhi banavta, tivare tooti jata.

• Aap ye safar ghani kidi chhe. 1239 H. ma paanch mahina aap ye Surat, Khambat, Ahmedabad ane Kapadvanj ni safar kidi. 1240 H. ma aap Dahod, Ratlam ane Mandsor rahya. (1241 H. Mandsor ma aap na muqam darmiyan ghano fasad thayo. Aap na ane Mumineen na makano ne jalaya. Ek goli aap na qareeb si nikli gai. Khuda Taala ye aap ni hifazat kidi. Aakhir ma Angrez Lashkar ne bolayu, ane toofan qabu [control] ma aayu. Ujjain ma Mazharali ye fitnat kidi, te pakrayo. 1241 H. ma Ujjain ma Shaadi na jaman ma gosht pakavva ni babat ma qaumi riot thayu. Hinduo ye apna saath len-den band kidi. Angrez sarkar vachche pari ane saghlu chalu thayu. 1247 H. ma aap Kapadvanj, Ahmedabad, Sidhpur Jamnagar ane Mandvi padhara.

• 15 Zilqad 1252 H. 54 varas ni umare, Surat ma aap wafat thaya. Aap ni qabr mubarak Qubba Izziyah ma chhe. Aqa Moula t.u.s. aap ni nasal ma si chhe.

19- Urus: Syedna Idris Imaduddin AQ [19th Dai]

19 Zilqadul Haram - Syedna Idris Imaduddin (r.a.) (Yemen)

19ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Idris Imaduddin (r.a.)
najle Dail Ajal Syedna Husain (r.a.)
Vafat: 19 Zilqadul Haram 872 H. 1468 A.D. Shibam -Yemen

• Syedna Idris Imaduddin (r.a.) Aale Valid na 13ma Dai ane satar na doatma 19ma dai chhe. Aap ni shaan ghani azeem chhe.

• Daur-e-Mohammedi na aap Idris chhe. Aap Shebaam ma muqim tha.

• Aap ni teen shaan ma [1] Ilm,[2] Jihad ane [3] Hind ni taraf Dawat ni tamheed karva ni chhe.

• [1] Ilm Syedna Idris na mashoor kitabo ma # Uyunal Akhbar, # Nuzhatul Afqar, ane # Zohral Maani chhe.

• [2] Aap ye ghani jihad kidi chhe.

• [3] Hind ni taraf Dawat ni tamheed kidi.

• Yemen ma ek Hindi gareeb ayaa, aap ye tehne tarteeb ma 300 Hudud ni aagal bithaya. Yemen na logo ne bhaari lagu.

• Aap ye Hind na logo nu imtehan lidu. Hind na Vaali Moulai Adam a.q. ne tena ghar na saqqa (Servant) ni pachhal Namaz parhva nu farmayu. Mumineen saghla ye imtehan ma paar utra. Aap ye Dawat Hind ni taraf jaase tehvi peshangoi farmavi.

• Aap na zaman ma Yemen ma Cholera ni bimari felai. Aap na farzand, bhatija, ane ghana Hudud gujri gaya. Aap na Kakaji saheb Maad bin Syedna Abdullah r.a. bhi gujri gaya. Hind ma bhi Dawat par aafat aavi, Jafar ni fitnat uthi. Aap ye Dafeel Afaat ni doa lakhi farmavi, je apan aaj bhi roj parhiye chhe.

• Aap ni qabr mubarak Shibaam ma chhe.

• Aap na Qubba mubarak Syedna Ali bin Syedna Husain r.a. ye banaya, je Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin r.a. na zaman ma Zaidio ye tori nakha, ane vahan Store room banayu.

• Dail Ajal Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) ye aap na Qubba ni tameer farmavi ane Shawwal 1431 H. ma iftetah kidi..

21- Urus: Syedna Ali Shamsuddin AQ [22nd Dai]

21 Zilqadul Haram - Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a. najle Syedna Husain r.a. (Yemen)

22ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a.
najle Syedna Husain r.a.
Vafat 21 Zilqadul Haram, 933H. 1527 A.D. Masaar, Yemen

Mazoon:
23 ma Dai, Syedna Mohammad Izzuddin r.a.

• Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a. Aale Valid na 16ma Dai chhe.

• Dail Ajal, Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a. tamam Doat ul Mutlaqeen r.a. na darmiyan ghano kam [nery short] arsa Dawat na arsh par rahya chhe. sirf 41 divas.

• Aap nu laqab Shamsuddin chhe.

• Aap na aaba-e-Kiram ni jem aap ye bhi Khuda ni rah ma ghanu kharch kidu.

• Haq nu ilm nashara karva ma saye jaleel farmavi. Aap na Kakaji na dikra Syedna Mohammed Izuddin r.a. ye aap ne yaari didi.

• Aap Hisne Shibam ma muqeem rahya.

• Aap ni qabr mubarak Masaar pahar par chhe. Vahan teen Doat Mutlaqeen r.a. madfoon chhe.

22- Urus: Syedi Sadikali Saheb AQ bin Mulla Sultanali (Surat)

22 Zilqadul Haram - Allama, Syedi Sadikali Saheb a.q. bin Mulla Sultanali (Surat)

Allama, Syedi Sadikali Saheb a.q.
bin Mulla Sultanali
Vafat: 22 Zilqadul Haram, 1233 H. 1818 A.D. Surat
Zaman: 44ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin r.a.

• Allama, Syedi Sadikali Saheb a.q. ye teen Doat [ 42-43-44 ] ni khidmat kidi.

25- Urus: Syedna Ali Shamsuddin bin Syedna Hatim AQ [4th Dai]

25 Zilqadul Haram - Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a. (Yemen)

4tha Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a.
najle Dail Ajal Syedna Hatim bin Ibrahim (r.a.)
Vafat: 25 Zilqadul Haram, 605 H. 1209 A.D. Sana'a, Yemen
Daur-9 years
Maazoon-e-Daawat:
Syedna Ali bin Mohammad bin Valid r.a.

• Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a. ne Syedna Hatim (r.a.) ye Taalim ane Tarbiyat vaste Syedna Ali bin Mohammed Alvalid (r.a.) ni taraf sonpa.

• Aap nu laqab 'Shamsuddin' hatu.

• Dushmano na zulam na lide aap Huteb si Sana'a aaya, ane Sana'a ma aap na mzoon Syedna Ali bin Mohammed r.a. na ghare muqim rahya. Aap ne yahan bhi Sana'a na Yahburio ne samjhvva ni ghani koshis kidi magar temne Banu Yaab no samno karvo paro.

Dushmano ye Syedi Sabaa Shaheed (a.q.) na farzand Sultan Hatim ne qatal kida. Shaheed Hatim na qabila ni javabdari Syedna Ali r.a. ye mathe lidi, ane kai vadhare na bane te vaste aap khud Zimarmar ma banu Hatim na makan ma rahya.

• Yahan bhi dushmano ye aap na saath jang kido ane chhupi rite aap ne zehar pohnchayu.

• Aap bimar thay, to banu Hatim na logo aapne miyana ma san'aa lai gaya, jahan aap vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr mubarak ni san'aa ma jaga maaruf [known] nathi.

• Aap ye Syedna Ali bin Mohammed Valid ( r.a.) par nas farmavi.
