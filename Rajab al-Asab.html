Rajab al-Asab

02 - Urus: Seth Bhaijibhai bin Kadibhai (Karachi)

2 Rajab al-Asab - Seth Bhaijibhai bin Kadibhai (Karachi)

,

Seth Bhaijibhai bin Kadibhai Vafat: 2 Rajab al-Asab 1268 H. [1853 A.D.] Karachi

• Seth Bhaijibhai bin Kadhibhai ni viladat 1213 H. ma Mandvi [Kutch-india] ma thai.

• Naani umare Mandvi chhori Karachi vasayu. Pehla nokri ane pachhi vepar shuru kido.

• Logo ma aap ghana imandar, parhezgar, deendar, aabid ane ek sachabola insan tarike mashhoor thaya.

• Aap na be bairo hata, ane te beve na zhagda na lidhe aap ghano waqt Masjid ma gujarta.

[Aaj ye masjid Karachi ma Ibrahim Masjid tarike mashhoor chhe.]

• 2 Rajab 1268 H. ma Karachi ma cholera ma vafat thaya.

• Te waqt Karachi ma khas qabrastan nohtu, te si aap na khas makan si thore duur aap ne dafan kida.

• Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) yahan ziyarat kidi chhe.

• Aap na qabr par Roza chhe. Aap ni ghani mannat chale chhe.

4 Rajabul Asab Urus

37ma Dai ,Dail Ajal, Syedna Noormohammed Nooruddin r.a.
Najle Syedna Moosa Kalimuddin r.a.
Vafat: 4 Rajabul Asab 1130 H.
[1718 A.D.] Mandvi Daur: 8 yrs.

• Syedna Noormohammed Nooruddin r.a. 'Aale Bharmal' na chotha [4th] Dai chhe. Aap ye aap na jad ( Grand Father) ane vaalid Syedna Kalimuddin (r.a.) saheb( father) , beve Doat ni ghani khidmat kidi Syedna Kalimuddin (r.a.) na aakhari zaman ( Last days ) ma aap Jamnagar na raja Jam na zulm na sabab qaid ma tha

• Aap ne ghani musibato pohnchi chhe. Jam ye aap ni milkato lai lidi. Aap Jamnagar muki Morbi padhara.

Vahan na Raja ye aap nu isteqbal kidu

• Jamnagar ma Jam na baad ehna dikra Rajsingh ye aap ne pachha Nagar bulaya, ane aap ni saghli milkat pachhi sonpi. Aap Nagar padhara, ane Dawat na ealaam buland kida.

• Rajsingh ne ehna bhai ye qatal kido. Nagar na haalat bigri gaya. Aap pachha Nagar chhori Mandvi padhari gaya.

• Aap na zaman ma ghana bilad ma dukal (famine) paro. Gair logo Mumineen nu anaj ane maal asbab luti ne lai gaya. Syedna Nooruddin ye saghla ni taraf anaj ane chandi na sikka ( coins) mokla.

• Aap ek din Mandvi na qabrastan taraf padhara. Ehna baju ma ek zamin hati. Aap ye farmayu ke aa zamin kharidi lo, ek din kam aavse. Farmayu ke ye zamin ma aale Mohammed (s.a.) na Dai dafan thaase.

• 1130 H. na Shahre Rajab ni pehli tarikhe aap ye rozu kidu thu. Magrib na waqt aap ne gashye aayu.

Chothi raate aap vafat thaya. ane je zamin aap ye kharidvanu farmayu thu ehma aap dafan thaya.

• Aap na vafat na bara ma em zikar aave chhe ke aap dadar si giri gaya. Be din aap dard ni shiddat ma rahya.

• Aap na vafat na baad Panchmi taarikh aap na aqilat (wife) Fatema Aisaheba vafat thaya.

• Mandvi ma aap na aalishan nava Qubba Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin RA. ye haal ma banaya chhe.

Almaulal Ajal , Syedi Hasanji Badshah a.q. najle Syedi Shamskhan [a.q.]

04 - Syedna Noor Mohammed Nooruddin AQ [37th Dai]

Vafat: 4 Rajabul Asab 1102 H.1691 A.D. Ujjain

Zaman: 35ma Dai,
Dail Ajal,
Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a.

• Almaulal Ajal ,Syedi Hasanji Badshah a.q. Moulai Fakhruddin Shaheed ni nasl ma si chhe.

• Aap ye 32 ma Dai si 35 ma Dai tak, char Doat ni khidmat kidhi chhe.

• Aap ye ane aap na vaalid Syedi Shamskhan [a.q.] Saheb ye Ahmedabad ma Qaidkhana ma Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed r.a. ane Syedna Feerkhan Shujauddin r.a. ne jaman pohnchavta, ane kapra namazi karta.

• Syedna Feerkhan Shujauddin r.a. ye aap na vaste doa kidi, je na sabab Khuda Ta'ala ye aa khidmat no ajar ye aapo ke, aap ni nasal ma Doat Akrameen thaya. Dail Asr, Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) aap ni nasal si chhe.

• Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed na Shahadat ni zikar Syedi Jivanji Saheb ye 'Kitabul Tazkarah' ma likhi chhe. Apan ye zikar Syedi Sadiqali Saheb ye likhela Hadesa ma aksar parhye chhe.

• Aap 34ma Dai, Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a. bin Mulla Raj ni hazrat ma Jamnagar aaya.

Syedna ye jaheri ane bateni ilm si aap ne navaza. Aap ne ye sharaf hasil thayu ke aap ye Syedna Badruddin r.a., Syedna Zakiuddin r.a. ane Syedna Nooruddin r.a. na qadam mubarak dhoi ne paani pidu.

• Aaj mumineen, Ujjain ma aap na Mazar na Sahan ma je hoj chhe, tema si pani si voh barakat hasil kare chhe.

• Aap ye Gujarat ane Malwa na bilad ma Dawat na ealaam ne nashar kida.

• 1081 ma Syedna Zakiuddin [r.a.] ye aap ne Dawat vaste Ujjain mokla. 1102 H. ma aap vahan vafat thaya.

• Aap na Qubba Ujjain ma Bahadarpura ma chhe. Haal ma nava bana chhe.

• Sahan ma left ma hauz chhe, je nu paani ghanu barkati chhe.

• Aap na dikri Hayatibaisaheb ni qabr Ujjain na juna qabrastan ma chhe. Aap na farzand shk.

Mohammedbahi khanji ni qabr Surat ma Qubba Najmiyah ma chhe.

07-Urus: Syedna Shekhadam Safiyuddin RA (Ahmedabad)

7 Rajabul Asab - Syedna Shekhadam Safiyuddin r.a. (Ahmedabad)

28ma Dai,
Dail Ajal Syedna Shekhadam Safiyuddin r.a.
bin Miyan Taiyebshah Vafat: 7 Rajabul Asab 1030 H 1621 Ahmedabad Mazoon-e-Dawat: Syedi Alimohammad bin Firoz a.q.

• Syedna Shekhadam Safiyuddin r.a. ni viladat Baroda ma thai chhe.

• Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin (RA) ye aap no misaq lido. Aap shuru ma Baroda ma ilm parhta rahya, te baad Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin (RA) ni hazrat ma Ahmedabad ilm parhva aaya.

• Aap Hudud Fozola na saath Makkah padhara ane vahan si Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin (r.a.) ni hazrat ma Yemen aaya ane Dai ni khidmat ma rahya. Syedna Yusuf (r.a.) na vafat na baad aap Hind pachha vala chhe. Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah (r.a.) ye aap ne hadiyat na martaba ma charhaya.

• Aap ye chaar doat ni khidmat kidi. S. Dawood bin Qutubshah na zaman ma aap Deccan ma Dawat vaste padhara chhe.

• Gujarat na Sultano na taraf si zulm barhta rahya. Laeen Suleiman ni fitnat ma aap Syedna Dawood (r.a.) ni dhaal [ protection] bani ne khara rahya. Har imtehan par aap sabar karta.

• Aap nu Sabre Azeem, Husnul Bayan ane Sheerin Zabani, aap na moajezat ma si chhe.

• Ahmedabad na Suba Shah Murad ye aap ne qaid kida. Aap na saath Almoula Ali Mohammed Bin Firoz (a.q.), Syedi Khoj bin Malek Saheb (a.q.) ane bija ghana Hudud tha.[ Aa saghla Qaidiyo, je aap na saath hata, te saghla ni qubur Moulai Hasan bin Moulai Adam na Roza si magrib (west) taraf jata aave chhe.

] Qaidkhana ma dushmano aap ne undha latkavi ne marta. Aap saghla ne, qaidkhana ma Jalalpur [ shayad aajnu jamalpur] na Mumineen jaman ane paani pohnchavta, ane kapra namazi karta.

• Suleman ni fitnat ma aa misal aap ye ghani janfeshani kidi. Aap ne aakhari umar ma sehat par ghano asar thayi gayo, aankh ni binai kam thai. Dushmano aap par taan karta.

• 75 varas ni umare, 7 Rajabul Asab 1030 H. ma aap Ahmedabad ma vafat thaya. Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) ye aap na nava roza ni tameer kidi.

12-Urus: Syedi Najamkhan Sirajuddin AQ (Aurangabad)

12 Rajabul Asab - Syedi Najamkhan Sirajuddin a.q. (Aurangabad)

Almoula,
Syedi Najamkhan Sirajuddin a.q.
Najle Syedna Feerkhan Shujauddin r.a.
Vafat: 12 Rajabul Asab 1094 H. 1683 A.D.
Aurangabad Zaman: 35ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a.

• Syedi Najamkhan Sirajuddin a.q. aalim tha, Ilm na darya tha. Ikhlas ane vafa ma ek tha.

• Aap ye Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed r.a. si Ilm lidu. Syedi Khanji Feer a.q. na aap ustad hata.

• Aap Mazoon na rutba ma hata. Koi asrar [ private vaat] aap si bija ne kehvai gaya, khata thai. 34ma dai, Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a. naraz thaya. Aap na saghla maraatib lai lida. Mafsuhiyat bhi lai lidi.

Aap ye sabar kidi.

• Hajumiya na logo ye, aa vaqea no faydo lai aap ne gumrah karva ni koshis kidi. Yahan tak ke aap ne Dai banavsu ehvi lalach aapi.

• Aap Ikhlas na saheb hata, javab dido ke Moula ye mara maratb lai lida, magar mane iman na dayra ma to rakho chhe ne? Aap ye saghla mukhalefin ne duur kida. Ek arsa tak aap par imtehan rahya.

• 35ma Dai, Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a., jivare Ahmedabad padhara. Aap ye Syedi Najamkhan a.q.

ni khata bakhshi didi.

• Aap ye ghani takalif uthavi chhe. Dushmano ye aap ne qaid kida, ane aap par ghano zulm kido.

Aurangzeb, 900 mumineen na saath, aap ne qaidi ni halat ma Ahmedabad si Ahmednagar lai gayo.

Aap na gala ma tauq hati, sakhat garmi si aap na sina par daag pari jata. Qaidi ni halat maj aap Aurangabad ma vafat thaya.

• Aap ni qabr par Qubba chhe. Mazar na qabrastan ma Syedia Jivanji a.q. na bairo, Ummul Mumineen, Buji Aai saheba, Syedia Jivanji a.q. na dikra Mulla Valibhai, ane Bija 17 Ahmedabad na shaheedo ni quboor chhe.

13-Ayyamul Beez

13-14-15 Rajabul Asab Ayyamul Beez

13-14-15 Rajabul Asab
Ayyamul Beez [Golden Days]

• Rajabul Asab ni 13-14-15 mi tarikh ne Ayyamul Beez kahe chhe. Aa teen din roza karva no hukam chhe.

• Rivayat ma chhe ke Umme Dawood na farzand Dawood ne Abbasi Khalifa Abu Davaniq ye qaid kida. Umme Dawood, Fatema, potana farzand ni rihai vaste tazarrua na saath Khuda Ta'ala na najdeek doa karta rahya.

• Ek roz Imam Jafar-us Sadiq a.s. ni hazrat ma aaya ane arz kidi. Te waqt Imam a.s. ye farmayu , ay Fatema, tame Doa-ul-Istiftah kem nathi parhta? Yeh Doa parhva na sabab Aasman ane Jannat na darwaza khuli jay chhe, ane Doa mustajab thay chhe.

• Umme Dawood ne Imam a.s. ye aa teen din na roza, ane 15 mi taarikh ni namaz, Das [10] Surat ane Doa ni kefiyat batavi.

• Umme Dawood ye te misal amal kidu. Khuda Ta'ala ye Umme Dawood ni Doa suni lidi. Ehni mushkil ne asaan kidi, ane Dawood ne qaid si chhuraya.



14-Urus: Moulai Yaqub bin Moulai Bharmal (Patan)

14 Rajabul Asab - Moulai Yaqub bin Moulai Bharmal (Patan)

Moulai Yaqub bin Moulai Bharmal a.q.
Vafat 14 Rajabul Asab, Patan, Gujarat

Moulai Yaqub ni zikar Syedna Taher Saifuddin Saheb r.a. aap na risala shareefa "Mafatihul-Yakutil-Hams" ma farmavi chhe.

• Aap aala seerat na saheb hata.

Shareeat na qayam karnarhata. Bhalai na kaamo karnar hata.

• Dawat na tamam mumineen na deen ni rusumaat ne zinda karvama koshis na karnar hata.

• KhudaTa'ala ye aap ne ek bhala ane nek farzand ataa farmaya., je nu naam Ishaq chhe.

• ye farzand ni tarbiyat aap khud ye kidi, taa ke ilm ma, adab ma, aap na maqam ma qayem thaya.

• Te baad Moulai Yaqub vafat thaya'Patan-gujarat ma aap ni qabr mubarak chhe.

• Moulai Yaqub bin Moulai Bharmal a.q. , Moulai Abdullah na inteqal na baad, Hind-Sind na Pehla Vaali qayam thaya.

• Dawate Hadiyah qayam karta rahya. Aap na kakaji na dikra, Syedi Fakhruddin shaheed A.Q. bin Tarmalji aap na madadgar rahya.

• Aap ye Syedi Fakhruddin A.Q. ne Vaaghar [aaj nu rajasthan] ni taraf mokla. Ek muddat daraz tak te saghla makhfi ma Dawat karta rahya. Daur kayo hato, teni dates ma ikhtelaf chhe.

• Moulai Yaqub Patan ma rahya. Aap ye pehli masjid Patan ma ane Petlad ma banavi. Aap ye aap na farzand Moulai Ishaq ne taiyar kida, ane aap na qayam muqam kida.

• Moulai Yaqub, aap Rajabul Asab ni 14mi taarikh Patan ma vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr mubarak Patan ma purana Qabrastan ma chhe. Vahan aap Miyan Mehboob na naam si mashhoor chhe.

• Aap na qubba Purani ghat na Gujarati taraz na bana hua chhe. Vahan na muslim mujavar bairao ne ziyarat karva jaava deta nathi.

• 43 ma Dai na Zaman ma, Sheikh Ibrahim 'Saifee' ye aap ni ziyarat kidi chhe. Syedna Taher Saifuddin Saheb r.a. ye bhi yaha ziyarat kiddi chhe, tem vahan na juna mujavr kehta hata.

• Dai Al Asr Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) bhi Ziyarat vaste padhara chhe.

• 1433 H ma Apna Dai Syedna Mufaddal Saifuddin TUS bhi yahan ziyarat karva padhara chhe.

51ma Dai ,Dail Ajal,
Syedna Taher Saifuddin Saheb r.a.

[1965 A.D.] Mumbai

• Ibtedai Taalim Syedi Abdeli Mohyuddin a.q. si lidi. yej misal aap ye Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a. pase si bhi ilm lidu. • 28 varas ni umare aap Dawat na arsh par biraje chhe. Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a. ye farmayu tem" fitnat na vaadal gheraya hua chhe." Dushmano ye muqaddama bazi shuru kari did chhe. Itni lambi zabani thai ke Mumbai High Court ma koi ni thai nathi. Aap tamam dushmano no muqablo kare chhe. Tamam case ma Fateh Mubin hasil kare chhe. Saathe Saathe Mumineen na nufus taiyar kare chhe.

• Aap ni hayaat mubarak ma aap ye 49 Risala tasneef farmaya, je ma Zaahir, Baatin, Hikmat, Haqeeqat, Shariat, Fikeh, Taarikh, Hamd, Salavat, Doa, Naseehat, Qasaaed, Munajaat, Marasiyah na bayan farmaya. Har Risala ni nirali shaan chhe. Koi Risala ma 'Alif' nathi, to koi ma 'nukta' nathi ke koi ma 'fe' nathi.

• Aap ye 28 Munajaat ma 1216 Abyaat tasneef farmaya. 170 Qaseeda ma 9996 Abyaat likhi. Aap ni hayaat mubarakah ma, aap ye, 4197 Bayan farmaya, je ma Arabic, Urdu, Hindi, Gujarati and Lisane Dawat shamil chhe. Aap ni hayaat mubarakah ma, aap ye 3180 Sabaq parhaya. Ek din ma aap 28 sabaq parhavta. Aap moula ye 50 Asharah kida, ane ulum ni naher jaari kari didi. Shohoda-e-Karbala ni shaan ma 'Ya Syedas-Shohadai ' jeva Marasiyah likha-1358 H. Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed r.a. ni shaan ma ' Ya Qutba Deenillahe' na Marasiyah likha.

• Ajab shaan ni 'Jamea Saifiyah' banavi, senkro madresa khola. Mumbai ma Gurratul Masajid, Mecca ma Qasr-Saifee, Qaba na andar na parda (Kishwat Mubarakah), Amirul Muminnen a.s. ni zarih mubarak, Imam Husain a.s. ni zarih mubarak, Syedi Fakhruddin saheb r.a. na Qubba, ane Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed r.a. Qubba ane ye misal ghani imarato aap ye taamir farmavi.

• Haj vaste padhara. Gadeer-e-Khum na moze ni taeen farmavi. 1355 H. Karbala, Najaf Qahera, padhara. Doat ma aap pehla hata, je 830 varas na baad Jamea Azhar ma Imamat si Namaz parhavi [1355 H.] Aeroplane ma safar kidi, ane safar darmiyan namaz parhavi.[1373 H.]

• Shaam padhara ane Maulatena Sakina a.s. ni turbat ni tehqeeq faramavi -1355 H Askalaan ma Rasul Husain {a.s.} na madfan ni jagah ni tehqeeq farmavi-1359H.

• 9 din ni Asharah ni Waez shuru kidi -1337 H. Madina ma Asharah kida -1356 H. Har Khamis (Thursday) na din "Majalise Moayyedia" ni Waez farmavi.-Shuru kidi...1346 H.

• Rajabul Asab 1385 H. ni 19mi raate Matheran ma aap malaa-e- aala ni taraf sidhara. Aap ni umr 80 varas ni thi. 53 varas Dawat ni khidmat kidi.Mumbai ma aap madfoon chhe. Rozat Tahera ajab shaan na tameer thaya hua chhe.

20- Urus: Mazoon e Dawat il Haq Syedi Qasim BS Hakimuddin Saheb (DM) (Surat)

20 Rajabul Asab - Mazoon e Dawat il Haq Syedi Qasim BS Hakimuddin Saheb (DM)

Al Syyedul Ajal Wal Humamul Mubajjal Seqatut Dawatut Tayebiyah Mazoon e Dawat il Haq Syedi Qasim BS "Hakimuddin" Saheb (DM) Viladat: 16 Rajab-ul-Asab 1350 Vafat: 20 Rajabul Asab 1439 H. Surat

• Aap 51 Ma Dai Syedna Taher Saifuddin (RA) Na Shezada Che • Aap Na Maa Saheba (Mother) Nu Naam Ummul Mumineen, Khaleshato Naiebe Amirul Mumineen, Al Muqaddasa Fatema Aaisaheba Che.

• Aap 52 Ma Dai Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin (RA) Na Bhai Che.

• Maulana Muqaddas (RA) Sheqtu Dawatul Taiyyebiyah Na A'ala Khitab Si Nawaza Hata Ane "Hakimuddin" Laqab Inayat Farmayu Hatu.

• Aap Dawat Na Ilmi Ruye "Al Alimul Bare'aa " Ni Sanad Ane Dunyavi Taalim Ma Ruye "M.A" Ni Degree Hasil Kidhi Che.

• Hijri 1407 Ma Al Muqaddas Amirul Jamea Shz Syedi Yusuf Bhai Saheb Najmuddin Vafaat Paya Baad Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin (RA) Ye Al Jamea Tus Saifiyah Na Chaar (4) Omoraul Jamea Qayam Farmaya Tehma Aap Syedi Saheb Ek Che. Saifo - Burhan Zaman Ma Aapye Al "Vazar Tus Saifiyah" Ane "Sigtul" Amalat Na Chief Tarike Khidmato Anjaam Didhi Che Ane Aaj Bhi Khidmato Ma Daema Qaema Che.

• Syedi Saheb Na Bayaan Mumineen Ma Ghanaj Maqbul Che. Aap Hamesha Hazarat Aaliyah ma A'ala Tasuwraat Araz Karine Mumineen Na Jaano Ne Valiyullah Ni Ma'refat Vaste Taiyaar Karta Rahiya Che.

• Aap Ne Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin RA Na Milaad Na Deen 20 Rabbi-Ul-Akhar 1439 (Sunday) Ma 53ma Dai Syedna Mufaddal Saifuddin (TUS) Aqa Ye "Mazoon E Dawatul Haq" Na A'ala Rutba Ma Qaim Farmaya. Tiware Waaz Ma Taqbira Naraa Baland Thaya.

24-Urus: Syedna Abdul Mutalib Najmuddin AQ bin Syedna Mohammed bin Hatim AQ [14th Dai]

24 Rajabul Asab - Urus

14ma Dai, Dail ajal
Syedna Abdul Muttalib Najmuddin [r.a.]
najle Syedna Mohammed bin Syedi Hatim
Vafat: 24 Rajabul Asab 755 H.-1354 A.D.
Zimarmar-Yemen Doat no Daur: 9 varas- Umr- 85 years Mazoon-e-Daawat: Syedna Abbas (r.a.) bin Syedna Mohammed r.a.

• Syedna Abdul Muttalib Najmuddin [r.a.] Aale Valid na aathma [8th] Dai chhe.

• Aap Zamarmar ma qayam thaya. Dawat na nizam ne mazboot kido.

• Aap na bhai Syedna Abbas [r.a.] ye aap ni muavenat [ assist] kidi, jeh ne aap ye aap na mansoos banaya. • Aap ni Dawat ni himayat ma nirali shaan hati. Aap ni haibat [fear] dushmano par voh misal thi ke Sanaa na Sultan jang [war] no irado badli, aap na saath suleh karva par majboor thayo.

• Aap mashoor ravat [brave man] tha. Aap na saath Shaikh Hasan bin Syedna Ali tha.

• Aap ne Haj naseeb thai chhe.

• Rivayat ma chhe ke, "Jiv ni Bakri" ni rasam aap ye jaari kidi chhe.

• Aap 24 Rajabul Asab 755 H vafat thaya ane Hisne Zamarmar par dafan thaya chhe, Masjid na sahan ma, magar qabr nathi.

• Aap ni umr 85 varas ni thi.

Mukasire Dawat,
Syedi Qamruddin Bhaisaheb a.q.
Najle Syedna Hebatullah Moayyed Fiddin r.a.
Vafat:24 Rajab 1231 H. 1816 A.D. Ujjain
Zaman: 43ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a.

• Syedi Qamruddin Bhaisaheb a.q. Bava Mulla Khan na nabira chhe, ane 40ma Dai, Syedna Hebatullah Moayyed Fiddin r.a. na farzand chhe.

• Aap 42ma Dai, Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. ane 43ma dai, Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a. na Mukasir tha.

• 'Sahebe ashke Ali' tha. Koi sael [begger] aap na dar si pachho valto nohto.

• Aap Allama benazir tha. Aap ye Doat Kiram ane Hudud Fozalah ni akhbaar [history] "Ruwate Amnaa" ane "Saqaate Fozalah" si jame kari tasneef farmavi chhe.

• Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a. na zaman ma aap vafat thaya, 24 Rajab 1231 H. 1816 A.D.

• Aap ni qabr Qubba Najmiyah, Ujjain ma chhe.

• Aap ni 'masoor' ni mannat levay chhe.

26-Urus: Syedna Abdulqadar Najmuddin R.A (Ujjain)

26 Rajabul Asab - Syedna Abdulqadar Najmuddin R.A (Ujjain)

47ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Abdulqadar Najmuddin r.a.
najle Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin r.a.
Vafat: 26 Rajabul Asab 1302H. 1885 A.D. Ujjain Daur 45 yrs

• Syedna Abdulqadar Najmuddin r.a. ni viladat Mumbai ma 2 Shahrullah 1229 H.[1814] A.D. ma thai. Aap na Vaalid Saheb Syedna Zainuddin (r.a.) ye aap nu naam Yusuf rakhu hatu, Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin [r.a.] ye te badli ne Abdul Qadar rakhu.

• Aap na maa Saheba Mariyam Aaisaheba binte Mulla Noormohammed [Vafat 1232 H.] Bawa Mulla Khan ni nasal si chhe. Aap Ibne- Mariyam [Masihuz-zamaan] kehvata tha.

• Aap Aalim benazir tha. Aap ye Sironj ma Asharah kida. Thoru bayan Dawat ni zaban ma ane thoru urdu ma. Koi ne farq na lage , ye tarah bayan kidu.

• Hind na Doat ma aap pehla Dai chhe jeh ne Hajj nasib thai chhe. Aap Malwa ane Gujarat na ghana bilad ma padhara. Aap Kapadvanj padharela chhe.

• Aap na farzando ni shadi ma Angrej Sarkar ye 19 top ni salaami aapeli. Sarkar ma Aap nu ghanu maan hatu. Sarkar ye Aap ne court ma hazir rehva si 'Exemption' aapelu. ane 'First Class Sardar of Decaan' no khitab aapelo.

• Aap na zaman ma hazaro mumineen business vaste Africa ni safare nikla.

• Aap na dushmano ghana sakhat, ghana na aqida bigara. Aap ni nas no bhi inkar kido, ane fitnat peda kidi. Aap ye khamoshi ikhtiyar kidi.

• Ujjain ma 'Kumbh Mela' na darmiyan bimari felai, te ma Aap ne bhi asar pohnchi. Aap 73 varas ni umare bimari ma vafat thaya. Aap na Qubba Ujjain ma chhe, ane ek namunedar imarat chhe.

29-Urus: Syedi Luqmaanji bin Syedi Dawood bhai AQ (Udaipur)

29 al-Asab - Syedi Luqmaanji bin Syedi Dawood bhai a.q. (Udaipur)

Mazoon e Dawat, Syedi Luqmaanji bin Syedi Dawood bhai a.q.
bin Sheikh Dawoodbhai bin Mulla Mehmoodji a.q.
Vafat: 29 Rajab 1177 H. 1764 A.D.- Udaipur
Zaman: 40ma Dai, Syedna Hebatullah Moayyed Fiddin r.a.

• Mazoon e Dawat, Syedi Luqmaanji bin Syedi Dawood bhai a.q. ye Syedna Ismail Badruddin {saani} [r.a.] si Syedna Moayyed [r.a.] tak teen Doat ni khidmat kidi. Aap na ghana imtehan thaya.

• Syedna Ismail Badruddin {saani} [r.a.] ye aap ne Udaipur na farman kida. Syedna Moayyed r.a. ye aap ne Mazoon na rutba ma qayam kida.

• Aap hamesha panche [5] waqt namaz masjid ma parhta. Logo ne masjid ma aava vaste taqeed karta, ane khud bolavva jaata.

• Aap Aalim-e-Mahir [ Expert Scholar] tha. Jeva Arabic ma mahir tha, ej misal Urdu, Hindi, Sanskrit, Gujarati ane Mewadi zaban na mahir tha. Har zaban [language] ma aap ye shayeri kidi chhe. Aap nu takhallus "Jiv" hatu.

• "Hamd Rabbana" ane "Chaalis Sikhaman" [ 40 Advice] aap na mashoor tasnif [literary work] chhe.

• Rajab vaste aap lakhe chhe: 'Rajab aaya....dahada namaz na aaya'.

• Aap na vaalid maajid Shk. Dawoodji bin Mehmood [madfoon Udaipur] Hudud ma si tha, ane Doat Badriyen ni khidmat kidi chhe.

• 29 Rajab 1177 H. Aap Vafat thaya.

• Aap na mazar Udaipur ma gaam ma chhe. Haal ma ghanu tameer thayu chhe. Zaereen ne Rahishgah bhi ghani comfortable chhe.

• Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) na vaaleda Husaina aai shaeba Syedi Lukmanji ni nasal ma si chhe.
