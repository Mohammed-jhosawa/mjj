RABI AL-AWWAL

02- Urus: Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin R.A (Ahmedabad)

2 Rabiul Awwal - Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a. (Ahmedabad)

29ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a.
najle Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a.
Vafat 2 Rabiul Awwal 1041 H. 1632 A.D. Ahmedabad

Mazoon-e-Dawat: Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a.
Mukasir-e-Dawat: Syedna Qassimkhan Zainuddin r.a.

• Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a. ni viladat 18 Safarul Muzaffar 972 H. ma thai. Aap na valeda Rani Aaisaheb binte Ali bin Jivabhai chhe.

• 26ma Dai, Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah r.a. ye aap ne Hadiyat na martaba ma charhaya.

• Suleiman ni fitnat na waqt Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a. ye pardo ikhteyar kido, te waqt aap, Syedna Zakiuddin saheb r.a. aap ni khidmat ma rahya.

• Aap nu bolva nu ghanu mithu hatu. Aksar Haakim ne milta, ane Dawat vaste khair khwahi karta.

• Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a. jivare Lahore padhara, tivare aap Ahmedabad ma thehra, ane Moulai Ameenji bin Jalal ne yaari aapta rahya..

• Aap na zaman ma Aliya Firqa ni shuruaat thai.[1627 A.D.]

• Aap ye Dail Ajal Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a. bin Syedi Hasan bin Idris r.a. par nas kidi. Syedna Ali Yemen ma tashreef rakhta hata, ane vahan muqim rahya.

• Syedna Zakiuddin 2 Rabiul Awwal 1041 H. Ahmedabad ma vafat thaya. Aap ni umr 69 varas ni hati.

• Aap na nava Qubbat, Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) ye 1417 H. ma fari si tameer farmaya.

04 Urus: Moulai Habibullah bin Moulai Adam bin Syedi Bawa Mulla Khan A.Q (Ujjain)

4 Rabiul Awwal - Moulai Habibullah, bin Moulai Adam bin Syedi Bawa Mulla Khan a.q. (Ujjain)

Moulai Habibullah,
bin Moulai Adam bin Syedi Bawa Mulla Khan a.q.
Vafat: 4 Rabiul Awwal 1165 H. 1751 A.D. Ujjain
Zaman: 39ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Ibrahim Vajihuddin r.a.

• Dail Ajal Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed r.a. si Dail Ajal Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin Saheb r.a. tak ilm ni nehar pohnchavva ma je Hudud Kiram hata, te ma si Moulai Habibullah, ek chhe.

• Aap Dail Ajal Syedna Vajihuddin r.a. na madadgar tha. Syedna na vahan Shaadi na mauqa par aap ni vafat thai. Syedna ne ghano sadmo pohncho.

• Aap ni qabr Ujjain ma Saifee Mohalla ni Masjid ma chhe.

07-Urus: Syedi Abdeali Mohyuddin A.Q (Surat)

7 Rabiul Awwal - Syedi Abdeali Mohyuddin a.q. (Surat)

Almoula Ajal,
Syedi Abdeali Mohyuddin a.q.
najle Dail Ajal Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin r.a
Vafat : 7 Rabiul Awwal 1326 H. 1908 A.D. Surat
Zaman: 50ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a.

• Syedi Abdeali Mohyuddin a.q. Syedna Husamuddin r.a. na Shahzada ane Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a. na shafeeq biradar chhe.

• 47ma Dai si 50ma Dai tak char Doat ni khidmat kidi.

• Ilm ma aap no maqam azeem chhe. Arabic zaban ma aap na Marasiyah, fasahat ane balagat si bhara hua chhe.

• 47ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdulqadar Najmuddin r.a. si app ye ilm hasil kidu. 51ma Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. na aap mufeed [ustad] tha.

• Syedna A.Q. Najmuddin r.a. ni Mundra ni safar ma jivare safinu dariya ma garaq thava si bachi gayu, te waqt aap Dai na saath tha.

• Salamati ni masarrat [khushi] na aa mauqa par Syedna ye aap ne Hudud na martaba ma buland kida.

• Aap 7 Rabiul Awwal 1326 H. Surat ma vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr qubbat Izziyah ma chhe.

10- Urus: Syedna Abdullah Badruddin R.A (Surat)

10 Rabiul Awwal - Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a.(Surat)

50ma Dai, Dail Ajal,

Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a.

najle Dail Ajal, Abdulhusain Husamuddin r.a.

Vafat: 10 Rabiul Awwal, 1333H. 1915 A.D. Surat

umr: 71 yrs | Daur 10 varas

• Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a. ni viladat 18 Rajabul Asab 1262H, thai.

• Aap Punam na Chandra tha. Aap aale Tarmal na Dai chhe.

• Aap ye Syedna Najmuddin r.a., Syedna Husamuddin r.a. ane Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin r.a. em teen Doat ni khidmat kidi.

• Syedi Abdeali Mohyuddin a.q. aap na biradar chhe.

• 49ma Dai, Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin r.a. na Zaman ma Dawat nu den [debt] adaa karva vaste aap ye ghani janfesani kidi hati, vahan tak ke aap ne jamvani bhi fikar nohti..

• 1324H. ma Asharah Mubarakah ma aap ye pehlij waqt Waez farmavi.

• Aap ye ghana bilad ma safar kidi hati. Aap na zaman ma ghani Masjid tameer thai.

• Aap ni umr 71 varas ni hati. 28 Safrul Muzafaar, 1333H. aap ye Dail Ajal, Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. par nas farmavi, ane kahyu ke Bhai, Dawat par bhale taklif hoy, Imamuzzamn no haath aap na upar chhe.

• Aap Surat ma vafat thaya chhe, qabr mubarak Qubba Izziyah ma chhe.

• Aap ni qabr Surat ma Qubba Izziyah ma chhe.

12- Urus: Ummul Mumineen, Bu Saheba Amatullah Aai Saheba (London)

12 Rabi al-Awwal - Ummul Mumineen, Bu Saheba Amatullah Aai Saheba (London)

Ummul Mumineen,
Bu Saheba Amatullah Aai Saheba
Viladat 15 Zilhaj ul Haram 1338 H.
Vafat: 12 Rabiul Ula 1415 H. London
Zaman: Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA)

• Ummul Mumineen, Bu Saheba Amatullah Aai Saheba ni viladat 15 Zilhaj ul Haram 1338 H. ma thai. Aap Bhai Abdulhusain bin Taher Bhai Saheb najle Syedna Abdulqadar Najmuddin r.a. na nek dukhtar chhe. Aap na dadi, Ummul Mumineen, Ratan Aaisaheba, Aqeelate Dail Ajal Syedna Abdulqadar Najmuddin r.a. chhe.

• Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) na saath aap ni shaadi 27 Zilqad 1354 H. [ 19 Feb.1936 ] Surat ma thai.

• 1397 H. ma aka Moula R.A ye aap ne Hadiyat na sharaf si navaza, ane " Khalesato Naebe Ameeril Mumineen" na khitab si musharraf kida.

• Aap ye aka Moula R.A. na saath Haj, Umrah, Karbala Mualla, Misr, Shaam ane atbaate aliyah ni ziyarat nu sharaf hasil kidu.

• Aap ne Ziyarat no ghano shauq, jahan jay vahan sonu ane zavahir taqreeban arz karta.

• aka Moula R.A farmave chhe, [Hasanfeer-1429] "Amatullah Aai Saheba ghana yaad aawe che, Aap ni tarbiyat --farzando ne sikhayu ke kem Dai ni Hazrat ma khara rahe, kem salaam thai, Aap ye Ramazan ma hafti likhi"

• Deeni rasamo ne zinda karva ane ibadat ni mausam ma har meeqat par parhva vaste doa no zakhiro, aap ye collect kari chhapaya chhe, je 'Bu Saheba na sahifa' tarike olkhay chhe, te Sahifatus Salat, Doao -Syedna Moayyaed Shirazai r.a., Das Surat ni hafti, etc. aap na nayaab tohafa chhe.

• Aap sadaqo ghanoj karta. Pakavvano bhi itnoj shauq, aka Moula R.A aap na haath ni recipes ghana shauq si tanavul karta.

• Silai ane jwellaery no bhi itnoj sahauq. Koi bahen ye sonu na pehnu hoy to takor karta ke sun tijori ma mukva banayu chhe? Em kahiye ke aap ek " institution of ideal motherhood' tha.

• Daillah al amin ni ghani lambi muddat tak 'nemal qareen' bani nirali shaan si aap ye khidmat kidi, ane fida thaya.

• 'Qasre Aali' na farzando ni tarbiyat ma, Dawate Hadiyah ni khidmat ma, Mumineen, Mumenaat ni khairkhwahi ma, ane Ibadat ane tilavat ma raat din gujara.

• 12 Rabiul Ula 1415 H. Milade Nabi s.a. ni raate, aap London ma vafat thaya, ane vahanj madfoon chhe.

14- Urus: Syedi Miaji Mulla Taj Saheb (Umreth)

14 Rabiul Awwal - Syedi Miaji Mulla Taj Saheb (Umreth)

Almoula Miyanji bin Taj saheb, a.q.

Vafat: 14 Rabiul Awwal, 999 H. Umreth

Zaman: 27ma dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a.

• Aap 24ma Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. na Hudud tha.

• Char Doat [ 24-27 ma dai] ni khidmat kidi. Yemen jai ilm hasil kidu.

• Aap na biradar Moulai Musanji bin Taj Shaheed Baroda ma Roza madfoon chhe, ane bija be bhai [ Moulai Raj bin Taj ane Moulai Jafar bin Taj]- Jamnagar ma Shekh Feer na qabrastan ma chhatri ma madfoon chhe.

• Aap na roza Umreth ma chhe. Zaereen ghana aave chhe, ane potani murad puri kare chhe., khas karine skin disease vaste logo mannat le chhe.

22- Urus: Syedna Ali R.A bin Hanzala (Hamdan)

22 Rabiul Awwal - Syedna Ali r.a. bin Hanzala (Hamdan)

6tha Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Ali r.a. bin Hanzala

bin Abi Salim Mehfuzi Alwadaee
Wafat: 12 Rabiul Awwal 626 H 1229 A.D. Hamdan

Mazoon-e-Dawat:
Syedna Ahmed bin Mubaarak r.a.
Mukasir-e-Dawat:
Syedna Husain bin Syedna Ali r.a.
Daur: 14 varas

• 3rd Dai Syedna Hatim r.a. na zaman si aap Dawat ni khidmat ma ghani mehnat karta rahya chhe.

• Aap no zaman ilm no hato. Aap Ilm-e-Najoom [astrology] ma jabardast mahir [very expert] hata. Duniadari [worldly interests] aap ye muki didi hati. Badshah ane Ameer aap ne khara rahi ne taazeem aapta.

• Haqeeqat na ilm ma " Simtul Haqaeq" aap na kitab chhe.

• Hind ane Sind ni taraf aap ye Vulat [vaalis] mokla chhe.

• Hind ma te waqt Maulai Yaqub bin Bharmal Vaali-e-Hind hata.

• Aap na zaman ma Duniya ma ghana jung thaya. Aap tena si mehfooz rahya.

• Aap ni Umr ghani mohti hati. Aap ni qabr Hamdan ma chhe, magar jaga maaruf nathi.

22- Urus: Moulai Dawood bin Moulai Raj A.Q (Morbi)

22 Rabiul Awwal - Moulai Dawood bin Moulai Raj a.q. (Morbi)

Almoula, Moulai Dawood bin Moulai Raj a.q.
Wafat 22 Rabiul Awwal, Morbi

• Aap na vaalid Moulai Raj ye aap ne Ahmedabad na daras ma dakhil kidela. Patan si pachhi aap Moulai Raj na saathe Morbi qayam thaya.

• Har amar ma aap M. Raj na madadgar tha. Jafar ni fitnat na waqt aap ye ghani janfeshani kidi chhe. Moulai Raj a.q. na baad aap Morbi na Aamil thaya.

• Aap Morbi ma wafat thaya. Aap ni umr 100 varas ni haii. Aap ni qabr aap na vaalid M. Raj a.q. na ek baju ma chhe, jivare biji taraf aap na maa saheba Zulekha Baisaheba ni qabr chhe.

• Aap na farzand Moulai Adam a.q. [Jamnagar] Dail Ajal Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a. na jad [grandfather] chhe.

23- Urus: Moulai Raj bin Moulai Dawood A.Q (Morbi)

23 Rabiul Awwal - Moulai Raj bin Moulai Dawood a.q. (Morbi)

Almoula Moulai Raj bin Moulai Dawood a.q.
Wafat: 23 Rabiul Awwal 845 H. 1441 A.D. Morbi
Zaman 19ma Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Idris r.a.

• Almoula Moulai Raj bin Moulai Dawood a.q. aale Bharmal na nabira chhe. Vali-e-Hind Moulai Hasan bin Moulai Adam a.q. na farman si aap Patan [ Gujarat] na Aamil thaya.

• 835 H. si 950 H. no daur ghano mushkil gujro. Laeen Jafar ni fitnat shuru thai chuki hati. Mumineen na gharbaar ane maal milkat lutaya, kitna Mumineen shaheed thaya, ane Dawat na kitabo gum thai gaya. Aale Bharmal ni ghani taarikh, tena sabab, aaj milti nathi.

• Fitnat na aa mahaul ma, Moulai Raj Saheb ne aap nu watan Patan chhorvu paru. Dawat na kitabo, bane etla bachavi, potani jagir ane makan chhori, aap Morbi padhara. Vahan aap na vaalid Moulai Dawood bin Moulai Ali a.q. nu makan hatu, vahan qayam farmayo. Masjid ane kuvo banaya.

• Aap Morbi na pehla Aamil qayam thaya. Shariyat na aap ghana paband, koi waqt aap ni namaz fot thai nathi.

• Aap ni ek mashhoor rivayat parhiye: Aap kapra vanvanu [weaving cloth] kaam karta hata. Ek din thora kaam khatir taakheer [late] thai gai. Vuzu vaste aap ye kuva ma pani nikalva dol [ bucket] nakhi. Dol ma pani na badal Javahar aaya. Ghari ghari aam thayu. Aap ye istigfar kido, paani talab kidu, ane waqt par namaz adaa kidi. Aaj bhi voh kuvo Morbi ni Masjid ma chhe.

• Khuda Taa'la ye aap ne Javahar-Faani na badal Javahar-Qudsiyah bakhsha. Aap ni nasal ma nav [9] Doat, Mazooneen, Mukasereen, ane Hudud Fozola thaya.

• Aap ni Qabr Morbi ma chhe. Qubbat 2003 ma Earthquake ma damage thayela, Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA). ni raza mubarak si te fari tameer thaya chhe.

25- Urus: Syedna Ali Shamsuddin R.A (Hisne Afeda, Yemen)

25 Rabiul Awwal - Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a. (Hisne Afeda, Yemen)

30 ma Dai, Dail ajal,

Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a.

najle Syedi Hasan bin Syedi Idris bin Syedna Ali r.a.

Wafat: 25 Rabiul Awwal 1042 H. 1632 A.D. Hisne Afeda, Yemen

daur: 1 varas

• Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a. bin Syedi Hasan, Aal-e-Valid na 18ma ane aakhari Dai chhe. Rasulullah s.a.ni Doa mubarak na aakhari samar [ fruit] chhe, ane biji taraf aap Syedi Hasan bin Nooh Bharuchi a.q. ni nasal ma si chhe.

• Syedi Hasan bin Nooh a.q. ye Ilm ane maal, beve si Doat ni khidmat ma ghani janfesaani kidi chhe, je na sabab Khuda Ta'ala ye aap ni nasal ma Syedna Ali r.a. ni misal Dai bakhsha chhe.

• Hind ma Dawat naqal thaya baad 29ma Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Abdul Taiyeb r.a. ye Yemen ma aap ni taraf Nas mokli chhe. Te waqt aap Taybah ma tashreef rakhta hata. Ghani kam muddat aap Dawat na arsh par mustavi rahya. Aap ye dobara Hind ni taraf Syedna Qasimkhan Zainuddin r.a. par nas mokli chhe.

• Aap ni qabr mubarak Hisne Afeda ma ek alag Qubbat ma hati, je aaj maaruf nathi. Aap ni umar 69 varas ni hati.
