Ramazan ul Moazzam

02- Urus: Syedi Wali Bhaisaheb bin Syedi Habibullah (Parda, MP)

2 Ramazan ul Moazzam - Miyan Saheb Vali Mohammed [a.q.] (Parda M.P)

Al Haddul Fazil,
Miyan Saheb Vali Mohammed [a.q.]
Bin Sheikh Habibullah
Vafat:2 Ramazan ul Moazzam 1262 H. [1846 A.D.] Parda M.P.
Urs: 22 Shaban kare chhe.
Zaman: 47ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdul Qadar Najmuddin r.a.

• Miyan Saheb Vali Mohammed [a.q.] 50 ma Dai Syedna Abdullah Badruddin Saheb [r.a.] na nana thay chhe.

• Teen Doat ni khidmat kidi. Tamam Umr khidmat ma guzari. Doat Kiram ni madeh karta. 47ma Dai Syedna Abdul Qadar Najmuddin Saheb [r.a.] ni nas na shahedo [witness] ma aap ek chhe. Haj ni safar ma aap Syedna Najmuddin [r.a.] ni khidmat ma hata. Surat ma 1252 H. ma ghani mohti aag lagi thi, te waqt aap Syedna Najmuddin [r.a.] ni khidmat ma ghani Janfesaani kidi. Dawat na khusus kitabo ne bachaya.

• Aap Aalim Fazil tha. Ghana Rasail likha chhe, je ma "Risala Burhanuddin", "Risalatul Burhan", "Zu-Shahadatain", ane "Najmus Saqib" mashhoor chhe.

• 2 Ramazan 1262 H. aap vafat thaya . Qubba Parda [M. P.] ma chhe. Urs 22 Shaban thay chhe.

• Aap na vaalid Shk. Habibullah a.q., ane aap na farzand Shk. Ahmedali a.q. ni qabr Surat ma Begampura ma chhe.

08-Urus: Syedi Fazal Bhaisaheb Qutubuddin bin Syedna Abdullah AQ (Surat)

8 Ramazan ul Moazzam, Syedi Fazalbhai Saheb Qutubuddin (Surat)

Mazoon-e-Dawat,
Syedi Fazalbhai Saheb Qutubuddin
najle Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a.
Vafat: 8 Shaharullah 1351 H. Surat
Zaman: 51ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a.

• Syedi Fazalbhai Saheb Qutubuddin 50ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a. na Shahzada chhe. Aap nu laqab Qutbuddin chhe. Aap Aale Tarmal ma si chhe.

• Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. ye aap ne 1333H. ma Mukasir na rutba ma qayam kida, ane 1336h. ma Mazoon-e-dawat, Syedi Dawoodbhai Saheb Shahabuddin a.q. na vafat na baad aap ne Mazoon na rutba ma qayam kida.

• Aap ni qabr mubarak Mazar-e-Saifee, Surat ma Syedi Dawoodbhai Saheb ni chhatri na pachhal sahan ma chhe.

09-Urus: Syedna Abdullah Fakhruddin AQ bin Ali [16th Dai] (Yemen)

9 Ramazan ul Moazzam, Syedna Abdullah Fakhruddin [r.a.] (Yemen)

16ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Abdullah Fakhruddin [r.a.]
bin Maulai Ali Najle Syedna Mohammed
Vafat: 9 Shaharullah 809 H. 1407 A.D. Zimarmar, Yemen

• Syedna Abdullah Fakhruddin [r.a.] Aale Valid na 10 ma Dai ane satar na 16ma dai chhe. Aap nu laqab Fakhruddin chhe.

• Ghani jaddo- jehad ane Janfesaani si Dawat na aalam buland kida, ane zulm na logo si Mumineen ni hifazat kidi.

• Aap ek jabardast shayar tha. Waaz ane bayan ghani shaan si karta.

• Aap na mohta farzand Syedi Husain (a.q.) har amar ma aap na moavin tha. Chhoti umar ma itnu ilm hasil kidu ke bija olamao ne hasad thai. Kahe chhe ke Syedi Husain (a.q.) ne tamra ma zehar aapu. Aap ni vafat no Syedna Ali RA ne ghano sadmo pohncho chhe. Aap na bija farzand Syedna Hasan [r.a.] ne Haraz si Zamarmar laya, ane aap na janashin qayam farmaya.

• 794 H. ma aap na Shahzada Syedna Hasan [r.a.] ye Shibam no killo fateh kido, je fateh na mahina ma 19 ma Dai Syedna Idris [r.a.] ni viladat thai.

• Aap ye Sheikh Qazi bin Hasan bin Ali bin Pishar [r.a.] ne Hind ni taraf mokla. Hind ma Dawat na umur behtar qayam thaya.

• 85 varas ni umare, 9 Shaharullah aap vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr mubarak Zamarmar ma chhe.

16-Urus: Syedi Hebatullah Jamaluddin A.Q (Jamnagar)

16 Ramazan ul Moazzam - Syedi Hebatullah Jamaluddin (a.q.) (Jamnagar)

Mazoon-e-Dawat,
Syedi Hebatullah Jamaluddin (a.q.)
bin Syedi Shekhadam Safiuddin (a.q.)
vafat: 16 Shaharullah 1275 H. 1860 A.D. Jamnagar

• Syedi Hebatullah Jamaluddin (a.q.) aale bharmal na nabira chhe.

• Aap Syedna Moayyed (r.a.) na navasa, Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin (r.a.) na sasra ane Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin (r.a.) ane Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin (r.a.) na Bhatija chhe.

• Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin (r.a.) ye aap ne "Addurossameen" [La queemat jawahar] no khitab bakhsho chhe.

• Aap ye teen Doat Kiram ni khidmat kidi..

• 1272 H. ma aap ye Mumbai na Vaali ni khidmat kidi.

• Syedna Saifuddin (r.a.) ye aap ni shaadi aap na bahen ni Dikri Hayatibai binte Vazira Aaisaheba najlate Syedna Abdultaiyeb Zakiuddin (r.a.) na saath karavi aapi.

• Aap na vaaleda Allahrakhibai Saheba, Syedna Moayyed r.a.na shahzadi chhe.

• Aap 16 Ramazanul Moazzam 1275 H. Jamnagar ma vafat thaya.

17-Urus: Moulai Ahmed bin Ajabshah AQ (Ahemdabad)

17 Ramazan ul Moazzam, Moulai Ahmed bin Ajabshah [a.q.] (Ahemdabad)

Almoula,
Moulai Ahmed bin Ajabshah [a.q.]
Vafat: 17 Ramazan ul Moazzam 983 H. 1572 A.D. (Ahemdabad)
Zaman: 26ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah r.a.

• Moulai Ahmed bin Ajabshah [a.q.] 26ma Dai Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah na shafeeq biradar [Bhai] chhe.

• Aap ye Syedna Yusuf Njmuddin r.a. si Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah r.a. tak teen Doat ni khidmat kidi.

• Aap mohta vepari [businessman] tha. Haj par padhara, pachha aavta Yemen padhara, ane Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin [r.a.] ni hazrat ma hazir thaya.

• Syedna Yusuf r.a. na chehra par malal dekhi aap ye arz kidi. Syedna farmave chhe ke Hakim ye 3000 Ibrahimi sikka talab kida chhe. Nahi to mane qaid karse.

• Maulai Ahmed ye aap ni saathe je maal Hind si vechva vaste laya tha, te vechi ne 7000 Ibrahimi sikka Dai ni hazrat ma pesh kida. Aap ye ghani Doa farmavi.

• Aap 17 Ramazan ul Moazzam 983 H. Ahmedabad ma Vafat thaya.

• Aap ni qabr Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a na Qubba si aagal aavta chhatri ma chhe.

18-Urus: Syedi Ismail Bhaisaheb Badruddin A.Q (Surat)

18 Ramazan ul Moazzam, Syedi Ismail Bhaisaheb Badruddin a.q (Surat)

Mazoon e Dawat,
Syedi Ismail Bhaisaheb Badruddin a.q.
Najle Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin r.a.
Rehlat: 18 Shaharullah, 1325 H. [1907 A.D.] Surat
Zaman: 50ma Dai, Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a.

• Syedi Ismail Bhaisaheb Badruddin a.q., Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin r.a. na Shahzada ane Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin r.a. na biradar chhe.

• Aap Ikhlas na Saheb hata. Zaeefi na saath aap Masjid ma tashreef lavta. Hamesha Mashahid ane Majalis ma hazir rehta.

• Al Mazoon Syedi Hebatullah Jamaluddin a.q. na vafat na baad Syedna Najmuddin r.a. ye Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin r.a. ne Mazoon na rutba ma ane Syedi Ismail Badruddin a.q. ne Mukasir na Rutba ma Qayam kida.

• 48ma Dai,Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin r.a., 49ma Dai Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin r.a. ane 50ma Dai Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a. na zaman ma aap Mazoon na rutba ma hata.

• Aap vafat thaya te waqt Aap ni Umr 90 varas n fauq hati.

• Aap ni Qabr Qubba Izziya ma chhe.

19- Urus: Syedna Mohammed Izziuddin AQ bin Syedi Jiwanji [44th Dai](Surat)

19 Ramazan ul Moazzam, Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin r.a. (Surat)

44ma Dai, Dail Ajal
Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin r.a.
najle Maulal Ajal Syedi Jivanjee (a.q.)
Rehlat: 19 Shaharullah 1236 H. 1821 A.D. Surat

• Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin r.a. ni viladat 1203 H. ma thai.

• Aap nu Watan Aurangabad chhe.

• Moulana Izzuddin (r.a.) ane aap na biradar Syedna Zainuddin (r.a.) nu nasab ajab buland chhe. Ek taraf si Moulai Fakhruddin Shaheed (a.q.) taraf pohncha chhe, to biji taraf Moulai Abdul Qadar Hakimuddin (a.q.) ni taraf pohnche chhe.

• Aap na Jad Syedi Dawoodbhai ye teen Doat ni khidmat kidi chhe. Aap na vaalid Syedi Jivanjee ye Dawat ane Doat Kiram ni khidmat ma ghani Janfesaani kidi chhe. Aap na vaaleda Buji Aai Saheba chhe,( jem ni qabr Aurangabad ma chhe)

• Aap Dawat na arsh par mustavi thaya te waqt aap ni Umr shareef 29 varas ni hati.

• Dawat na arsh par aap ghani kam muddat (4 varas) rahya, magar Ehma ghani barakat ataa kidi. Ilm, hilm, akhlaaq, nutq, aql, jood, zehan, lehan, hifz, vaaz, em tamam umur ma aap Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin (r.a.) na misal tha.

• 1234 H (1818 A.D.) ma British Govt. na koi documents gum thaya tha. Mumbai na Governor Lord Elphinston ye aap si madad talab kidi, ane aap ye bataveli saaye mutabiq voh dastavez mili gaya. Hukumat ye aap ni ghani izzat kidi.

• Aap ye Surat ma Qubba Najmiyah na samne makan kharidu, jagah saaf karavi. Aap na amal ma hikmat hati.

Aap vafat thaya, te waqt aap ne voh jagah ma dafan karva ma ayaa.

• Aaj vahan Qubba Izziya banela chhe, ane Aale Mohammed s.a. na panch [5] Doat Kiram madfoon chhe.

• 19 Shaharullah 1236 H. ma aap 33 varas ni umare vafat thaya. Qubba Izziya ma aap madfoon chhe.
