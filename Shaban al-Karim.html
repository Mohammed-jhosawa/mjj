01-Urus: Syedna Hebatullah Moayyed Fid din Saheb R.A (Ujjain)

01 Shaban al-Karim - Syedna Hebatullah Moayyed Fid din Saheb R.A (Ujjain)

01 Shaban al-Karim - Syedna Hebatullah Moayyed Fid din Saheb R.A (Ujjain)

• Syedna Hebatullah Moayyed Fid din Saheb [r.a.] ni viladat 16 Rabiul Awwal 1125 H. thai. Syedi Abdul Qadar Hakimuddin [a.q.] { Burhanpur } na aap pota chhe.

• Aap pehla Dai chhe, je Saheb ye Gujarati zaban ma nasihato ane nazam likhi chhe. Syedna Moayyed r.a. ye Ajabbu Baisaheba par ghana purdard noha likha chhe.

• Mogul Shahenshah Shahalam ye aap na vaste ek farman sadar kidu ke, aap "Mukhtiyar Qazi" chhe. Holkar ane Sindhia Sarkar ye aap ne Maan-Ikram-Jagiro aapeli, ane adalat ma Mumineen saame case na thai sake, ehva hukam nikalela.

• Aap ni shaan nirali chhe. Aap Surat padharela tivare vahan na hakim ye aap ne eeza pohnchavi, ane qaid karva sochu.. Aap Surat chhori ne Bilimora padhara. Te Qabal Surat ma Navlakhi Shahukar ye ardhi raate aap ni behurmati kari, ane aap ni khidmat na kidi.

Aap Mulla Wahedbhai na makan ma raat chhupi rahya. Itihaas gavah chhe, ke Navlakhi, je ek zaman no lakhpati hato, tenu namnishan na rahyu, ane Mulla Wahedbhai ni shaan aaj saghla jane chhe. Bilimora na raja ye aap nu ikram kidu. Khuda ye ehne maalamal kari dido.

• Yej misal Aap ni Aurangabad ni safar ma vahan na hakim ye aap ne iza pohnchavi. Ehno farzand sakhat bimar thayo. Hakim ye tauba kidi ane Khuda ye ehne shifa bakhshi.

• Aap na zaman ma Laeen Majdooh ni fitnat barpa thai hati. Majdooh ye Imam hovano daavo kido.

• Aap varanmvar Dawat na bilado ma safar karta. Malwa, Gujarat, ane Kathiawar na ghana bilado ma aap padhara hata. Safar darmiyan Rampura ma aap bimar thaya, ane Ujjain padhara. Vahan 1 Shaban 1193 H. aap Vafat thaya. Aap ni umar 68 varas ni hati.

• Qubbat Najmiyah, Ujjain, ma aap madfoon chhe.



14-Fadhil Raat Shabb-e-Baraat

14 Fadhil Raat Shabb-e-Baraat

15 Shaban-ul-Kareem
Shab-e-Barat [Lailatun Nisf ]

• Amirul Mumineen a.s. je char raat na ahya'[ jagva] vaste farmayu chhe, te ma si [Lailatun Nisf ] ek raat chhe.

• Fatemi Aimmat na asare maimoon ma Qahera ma Char raato 'Layalil Wuqud' si olkhati hati. Ye char raato ma si ek Shaban ni 15mi raat chhe.

• Te Zaman ma ye raato ma roshni karva ma aavti, te si ye raato ne 'Layalil Wuqud' kehva ma aave chhe.

• Ye raat ma Jamea Azhar ma Imam a.s. tashreef raakhta. Mumineen maukab [ procession] ma chalta hua Imam a.s. ni hazrat ma aavta. Fakhir jaman thata. Sona ane chandi ni bukhurdanio ma bukhur ni khushbu mahekti. Tamam Masjid ma roshni karva ma aavti. Aa raato ni roshni dekhva vaste duur daraz si logo Qahera aavta.

• Mumineen aa raat ma Mavali Kiram si Namaz ni shakelat nu bayan aayu chhe, te misal, 14 rakaa't Vasheq parhe. Har ek rakaat ma 'Alhamd' 14 vaar, 'Qul hovallaho Ahad' 14 vaar, 'Qul Auzo berabbil Falaq' 14 vaar, 'Qul Auzo berabbin Naas' 14 vaar, ane 'Aayatul Kursi' 1 vaar. Aa misal 14 raka'at tamam kida pachhi, vasilo lai doa kare.

• Niyyat: Usalli salata hazehil lailattil mubarakate ssharifate, laylate nisfe min shahre shabanal karime, arbaa asharata rakatan lilalahe azza vajalla.

15 Shabanul Karim - Syedna Hasan Badruddin r.a. (Masaar, Yemen)

20ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Hasan Badruddin r.a.
Najle Dail Ajal, Syedna Idris Imaduddin r.a.
Vafat: 15 Shabanul Karim 918 H. (1512 A.D.) Masaar, Yemen

Daur: 45 yrs 8 mahina 26 din.

20ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Hasan Badruddin r.a.
Najle Dail Ajal, Syedna Idris Imaduddin r.a.
Vafat: 15 Shabanul Karim 918 H. (1512 A.D.) Masaar, Yemen

15-Urus: Syedna Hasan Badruddin r.a. (Masaar, Yemen)

• Syedna Hasan Badruddin r.a. Aale-Valid na 14ma Dai chhe, ane satar na 20ma dai chhe.

• Aap 19ma Dai Syedna Idris [r.a.] na Shahzada chhe.

• Aap no zaman saadat no zaman hato. Aap no koi waqt ibadat si khali nohto. Fazal, diyanat ane kamal na saheb hata. Talabul ilm par shafaqat farmavta.

• Hind si Mumineen aap ni taraf hijrat kari ne aavta. Aap saghla par lutf ni nazar farmavta. Aap ye Hind na Mumineen no imtehan kido. Yemen si ek adna Mafsuh ne Hind ni taraf mokla. Umurud-deen ni raza ehne sonpi, ane Hind na mumineen ne ehni ta'at karva no hukam kido. Saghla ye ta'at kidi. Ye Mafsuh te baad Ahmedabad ma muqim rahya, ane vahan wafat thaya, jehni qabr Syedna Shekhadam Safiuddin na Qubba mubarak si Magrib taraf, aavta Burhani Masjid na najdik chhe.

• Aap na kakaji na dikra Mazoon-e-Ajal Abdullah bin Ali [r.a.] ye Dawat na amar ma Syedna ne yaari didi, ane Asadul Qavi ni misal saath rahya. S. Abdullah na vafat na baad aap ye aap na bhai Syedna Husain [r.a.] ne Mazoon na rutba ma qayam kidha, ane nasse jali farmavi.

• Yemen na Doat Kiram ma aap saghla karta ziyada arsa [45 years] tak Dawat na arsh par mustavi rahya.

• 15 Shaban 918 H. aap vafat thaya. Masaar na qilla par aap ni qabr chhe.



16-Urus: Syedna Ibrahim bin Husain AQ [2nd Dai] (Yemen Hamdaan)

16 Shaban ul Karim - Syedna Ibrahim bin Husain, Alhamedi [r.a.]

2nd ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Ibrahim bin Husain, Alhamedi [r.a.]
Vafat: 16 Shaban ul Karim 557 H. 1162 A.D. Gail Bani Hamid, Yemen
Mawazeen-e-Dawat:
Syedi Ali bin Husain bin Ahmed bin Valid r.a.,
Syedna Hatim bin Syedna Ibrahim Alhamedi r.a.
Mukasir-e-Dawat:
Syedna Mohammad bin Taher Al Harisi r.a.

• 2nd Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Ibrahim bin Husain, Alhamedi RA 'Kanzul Valad' na Saheb si mashoor chhe, je Kitab Haqeeqat na ek aala Kitab chhe.

• Ilme Haqaeq [ Haqeeqat mu ilm] na ulum ma aap inteha ne pohncha hata. Tena upar si aap ye vasheq ni namaz ni shaqelat taiyar kidi. Shahre Rajab ni pehli Raat, 27mi Rajab nii Raat, Shahre Shaban ni 15mi Raat, Shaharullah ni teen Fazil Raat, Lailatul Qadar, Idul Fitr, ane Idul Adha ni Raat ma Vasheq ni namaz qayam farmavi. Te Raato na Fazal ane Sharaf ne aashkar kidha. Teni maana ane Mamsool ne olkhaya. Tehni Rakato ane Duao ma hikmat na motio puraya. Aaj apan yeh vasheq ni namaz parhi je barakat hasil kariye chhe, te Syedna Ibrahim [r.a.] ni yaadgar chhe.

• Aap ye Syedi Ali bin Husain Alvalid ne Mazoon qayam kida, je ilmo amal na lehrata sagar tha. Deen ane Duniya na umur ma aap na madadgar [helper] tha. Syedna ne potani buzurgi [ old age ] na sabab Syedi Ali bin Husain no saharo tho, magar maut ye Syedi Ali ne jaldi bolavi lida. [ Vafat 554 H. ] Te baad aap ye aap na farzand Syedna Hatim r.a. ne Mazoon na rutba ma qayam kida. Ane Mukasir na rutba ma Syedna Mohammed bin Taher a.q.[ Bavisa na Saheb] ne qayam kida.

• Dai, Mazoon ane Mukasir na rutba ma aavi Shaanvali Shakhsiyat [ personalities ] na vujud si Dawat ni shaan dubala thai. Te dekhi Dushmano jali uthe ehma ajab sun? Zaidiyo ye Syedna Ibrahim [r.a.] ni deeni koshisho tuti jay te vaste ghani koshish kidi, magar kai chalu nahin. Chare taraf Imamuzzaman [s.a.] ni barakat aam thai. Dawat ma bahar aavi.

• 16 Shaban 557 H. aap vafat thaya. AAP YE Syedna Hatim r.a. par nas farmavi.

• Aap ni qabr Gail bin Hamid ma chhe. Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin [t.u.s.] jivare 1381 H. ma Yemen padharela, tivare Hamdan si aap 7 miles chalta hua aavi ne Gail Bani Hamid padhara hata, ane aap ni Qabr ni maarefat karavi hati.

• Aaj bhi dushmano aap ni qabr ni behurmati kare chhe, ane varamvar tehne tori nakhe chhe.

• Yahan ni Masjid ma Moula ye Yemen ni pehli safar 1381 H. ma Magrib ane Isha ni namaz parhavi chhe.

• 1432 H. ma aap ye yahan nava Qubba ni iftetah kidi hati, ane hazaro Mumeenin hazir thayela.

• Aap na zaman ma [547 H. 1154 A.D.] Rasul Husain ne Askalan si Qahera lavvama aaya.

•

19-Urus: Syedi Saleh bhaisaheb Saifuddin (Mumbai)

19 Shabanul Karim - Syedi Salehbhai Saheb Safiuddin a.q.(Mumbai)

Mukasir-e- Dawat,
Syedi Salehbhai Saheb Safiuddin a.q.
Najle Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin [r.a]
Vafat: 19 Shabanul Karim 1414 H. 1994 A.D.
Zaman: 52ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA)

• Syedi Salehbhai Saheb Safiuddin a.q. Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. na shafeeq biradar chhe. Puri zindagi Dawat ni khidmat ma nisaar kidi.

• Aap Dawat na khazana na la-qeemat jauhar hata. Aap ni Waaz, Bayan, Takreero, Vasila, ane Doa ma ajab zaut najar aavto.

• Aap ni viladat 1318 H. [1900 A.D.] ma thai. Hadiyat [1339H], Syedna ane Mazoon saheb {haal na Dai] haj par hata tivare Hindustan na vaali 1355 H., Ilqab [S.T.D.] 1359 H., J.P. [1370H], Mumbai ni Amalat [1349] 65 varas tak Mumbai ni amalat karva no aap no record hato. 1378 H. ma aap Mukasir bana.

• Aap ni mohti khubi ye hati ke aap koi waqt thakta nahin. 97 varas ni umare bhi aap khud har amar ni tawalli karta. Waqt na ghana paband tha. Aap ni shakhshiyat [ personality ] ane tajrubo [ experience ] lajavab hata.

• Aap na haath par je taamir na kaam thaya chhe, teh nu list ghanu lambu thay. Khas taamir ma Mumbai ma Saifee High School, Saifee Girls High School, Burhani College, Saifee Hospital, Mohammadi Baug, Badri Baug, Iwane Husaini, Taheri Hall, Husaini Masjid, Kurla Masjid, Sattad ni Masjid, ane Lonavla ma sanatorium, etc. chhe.

• Aap ne 5 Dikra ane 9 Dikri, 14 farzand hata.

• 97 varas ni umare, 19 Shabanul Karim 1414 H. aap Mumbai ma vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr Nariyelwadi, [Jamali Baug] Mumbai ma Mukasire Dawat, Syedi Ishaq Bhaisaheb na javar ma chhe.

22-Urus: Syedi Shaikh Valibhai bin Shaikh Habibullah (Paarda)

22 Shabanul Karim Urus

Ummul Doat ul Mutlaqeen,
Moulatena Hurratul Maleka Saheba r.a.
binte Syedna Ahmed r.a.
Vafat: 22 Shabanul Karim 532 H. -Zi Jiblah, Yemen umr: 96 yrs

• Moulatena Hurratul Maleka Saheba r.a. nu naam mubarak Maulatena Arwa binte Ahmed bin Mohammed bin Qasim Sulehi chhe.

• Aap ni viladat 440 H. ma thai. Aap ni tarbiyat aap na kaki Maulatena Asma binte Shehab ye kidi. Ahle Yemen aap ne 'Bilquis e Sugra' si yaad kare chhe, kem ke aap fehmo aqal ane husnut tadbeer ma be-misaal tha. Aap na aawaz ma qudrati kashish hati. Aap qiraat [ speech ] ghani behtar parhta. Aap Katebah [ authoress ] tha. Akhbar ane tawarikh aap ne ghani yaad thi. Tanzil, Taawil ane ilme Hadees ma aap maher [expert] tha.

• Aap ni shaadi Syedna Ali bin Mohammed Sulehi na Shahzada, Dai Syedna Ahmed Mukarram r.a. na saath 461 H. ma thai. Aap ne char farzand hata. Mohammed, Ali, Fatema ane Umme Hamdan. 484 H ma Syedna Ahmed na Vafat na baad aap Yemen na Maleka thaya, ane 484 si 532 tak Maleka rahya.

• Aap Doat ne parda na pichhe si Sabaq parhavta. Ilm, Fazal, parhezgari, zohad ane ibadat na saath aap ni siyasat ma husn ane tadbeer ma latafat ehvi thi ke Khaso-aam saghla na dilo mohabbat si jiti lida.

• Aap ye Imam Mustansir Billah [a.s.] na Zaman si Imam Taiyeb [a.s.] lag char [4] Aimmat Tahireen [a.s.] na Zaman ma Dawat qayam kidi. Ilm ne nashar kidu. Syedna Lamak [r.a.] ane Syedna Yahya [r.a.] ye Ilm nashar karva ma aap ni musaedat kidi. Yemen ma aap Imam Aamir [a.s.] na Hujjat tha. Imam Aamir [a.s.] ye aap ni taraf purana rumal mokla, ane Shahadat no ishaaro kido. 531 H. ma aap ye Imam Aamir [a.s.] ni hazrat ma javaheraat na hadiya mokla. Imam [a.s.] na farman si Doat Muttaqeen na silsila ne jaari kida.

• 525 H. ma aap ye Sana'a ni Jame-Masjid no mashraqi [East] hisso ziyada kido, ane Deewar par Panjatan Paak [s.a.] ane aap na Zaman lag na Aimmat na naam mubarak "Kufi" khat [ letters ] ma likha je Mehfooz rahya, ane Dail-ajal Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) ye voh naam 1402 H. ma tehqeeq farmavi mojeza si jaher kida.

• 22 Shabanul Karim 532 H. ma Moulatena Hurratul Maleka (r.a.) ye Imam Aamir (a.s.) ni raza mubarak si aap na matha par nas no taj pehnayo.

• 22 Shabanul Karim 532 H. [1140 A.D.] Zi Jiblah [Yemen ] ma aap 96 varas ni umare vafat thaya. Zi Jiblah ni Masjid na javar ma aap ni qabr chhe. Aap na vafat na baad ghana moajiz jaher thaya. Dushmano ye aap ni qabr ni jagah babat eteraz [objection] kido.

Qabr mubarak khodwa ma aavi. Ehma si dastavez nikla ke qabr ni jagah Masjid si alag chhe. Aap ni Zarih Mubarak ne tori nakhi, je 1414 H. ma Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin R.A ye dubara taamir farmavi chhe.



Moulai Sheikh Fir bin Dawood Shaheed
Vafat: 22 Shabanul Karim -Ranpur- Gujarat
zaman: 24ma Dai S. Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. ane 25ma Dai S. Jalal Shamsuddin R.A

• 24ma Dai Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin Saheb [r.a.] ane 25ma Dai Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin Saheb [r.a.] na Zaman ma aap ne Ranpur ni khidmat sonpi hati.

• Jahan tak zinda rahya, Dawat ni khidmat kidi. Ikhlas na Saheb tha. Tabiyat ma karam tho.

• Ranpur ma aap Shaheed thaya chhe. Gam an bahar nadi kinare aap na Qubba chhe. Qubba aalishan bana hua chhe.

• Aap na siva yahan mazar complex Takht Peer saheb ane bija saat [7] Shaheed ni qubur chhe.

• Mazar ma masjid ane zaereen vaste rahishgah haal ma ghani develop thai chhe.

• Ranpur gam, je mazar si jara duur chhe, te ma je Masjid chhe. Masjid ma takhti hati, je aaj gayab chhe.

• 500 varas juni aa masjid Yemen na Doat 23rd Dai Syedna Mohammed Ezzuddin r.a. na zaan ma 932 H. (ruler was Sultaan Bahadur Shah Zafar) ma bani hati.

25-Urus: Syedi Shams Khan bin Syedi Yusufji (Surat)

25 Shabanul Karim - Syedi Shamskhan bin Syedi Yusufji a.q. (Surat)

Mukasir-e-Dawat, Almaulal Ajal,
Syedi Shamskhan bin Syedi Yusufji a.q.
Vafat: 25 Shabanul Karim 1076 H. 1666 A.D. Surat
Zaman: 34ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a.

• Syedi Shamskhan bin Syedi Yusufji a.q. Syedi Fakhruddin Shaheed ni nasal si chhe. 31ma Dai si 33ma Dai tak Teen Doat ni khidmat kidi.

• Aap ye ane aap na farzand Syedi Hasanji Badshah [a.q.] ye Dawat ni azeem khidmat kidi chhe. Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed Saheb [r.a.] ane Syedna Feerkhan Shujauddin Saheb [r.a.] ne Qaidkhana ma aap jaman pohnchavta ane kapra namazi kari aapta.

• Syedna Feerkhan Saheb [r.a.] ye aap ne Dawat vaste Ujjain mokla. Syedna Ismail Badruddin Saheb [r.a.] ye aap ne Jamnagar na atraf [area around] ma Dawat vaste mokla hata.

• 1066 H. ma aap ne Ahmedabad na Vaali banaya. Saath ma Syedi Hasanji [a.q.] ane Syedi Khanji Feer hata. Syedi Hasanji Saheb [a.q.] ne Ujjain ane Syedi Khanji Feer Saheb [a.q.] ne Udaipur ni Dawat na umur sonpaya.

• Ahmedabad ma Syedi Shamskhan [a.q.] ye jaan maal kharchi ne Dawat ni khidmat kidi. Gujarat na Haakim Mohabbatkhan no zulm ghano hato. Aap ye ghani taklif uthavi chhe. Gharbaar rehan [ girve] muka. Hajaro Rupiya kharach thaya. Mumineen ni Masjido ma Sunni maulvi namaz parhavta. Tena Vajifa na kharch ghana thaata.

• 1076 H. ma aap ye Karbala java no irado kido. Aap Surat padhara. Karbala, Surat si javatu. Aap ni saath aap na pota Abdur Rahim bin Mohammedali hata.

• Surat ma aap bimaar thaya. Moosa Khan bin Hasanji na ghar ma utaro hato. Bimari aap ne jaan leva [ serious] nikli.

25 Shabanul Karim 1076 H. aap vafat thaya. Aap ye Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed Saheb [r.a.] ye aap ne aapelu kafan aapvani vasiyat kidi hati.

• Surat ma Navapura ni Masjid na qareeb aap ne dafan kida. Surat ma Hudud ni qabr aap ni pehli chhe. Najdeek ma phool na kyara hata.

• Aaj aap ni qabr Qubba Najmiyah ma chhe. Bahar aap ni Masjid chhe.

27-Urus: Syedna Ali bin Mawla Mohammed al-Walid AQ [5th Dai] (Yemen)

27 Shabanul Karim - Syedna Ali bin Mohammed bin Valid Saheb R.A (Yemen)

5ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Ali bin Mohammed bin Valid Saheb [r.a.]
Vafat: 27 Shabanul Karim 612 H. (1215 A.D.) Ugmur [Haraz], Yemen
Mazoon-e-Dawat: Syedna Ali bin Hanzala r.a.
Daur -7 years

• Syedna Ali bin Mohammed bin Valid Saheb [r.a.] Utba bin Rabia Valid ni nasal ma si chhe.

• Utba ye Taif ma Rasulullah [s.a.] ne musibat na waqt 18 dana Angoor [ grapes ] na aapi ne khidmat kidi chhe. Rasulullah [s.a.] ye Doa farmavi , je na tufail ma Utba na khandan [ family ] ma 18 Doat thaya chhe.

• Aa 18 doat Aale Valid kehvay chhe.

• Syedna Ali [r.a.] aap Aale Valid na pehla Dai chhe.

• Satar na Doat ma aap no maqam taarikhi [ historical ] ehmiyat [Importance] rakhe chhe.

• Aap ni viladat Imam Aamir [a.s.] na Daur ma thai. Aap ye juhur na Zaman mubarak ni barakat lidi. Maulatena Hurratul Maleka [r.a.] no Zaman dekho.

• Syedna Zoeb [r.a.] si Syedna Ali bi Hatim [r.a.] tak chaar [ 4 ] Doat ni khidmat kidi.

• Aap na jad [ grand father ] Ibrahim bin Abi Salmah muttaqi hata. Imam Mustansir [a.s.] ni khidmat ma Misr gayela. Imam [a.s.] ye aap ne shifa bolelo pani no kunjo [ jug ] aapelo. Te kunja ni mitti Syedna Ali [r.a.] Mumineen ne shifa vaste aapta. Aap ye vasiyat kidi hati ke te mitti aap na vafat na baad aap ni qabr ma mukva ma aave.

• Bacchpan sij aap kadawar [strong], zaheen [ clever ] ane chalaak tha. Ibadat ane riyazat ma aap nu pallu bhari hatu. Hamesha masjid ne aabad karta.

• Haqeeqat ane Taawil na ghana kitabo aap ye tasneef farmaya chhe, je aaj bhi Dawat na khazana ma mehfuz [ safe ] chhe. Aap ni Doao, Qasida ane Munajaat bhi mashhoor chhe. Mumineen aaj bhi tehni barakat hasil kare chhe.

• Syedna Ali Dawat na arsh par 94 varas ni umare jalwanuma thaya. Aap na zaman ma Mohammed bin Ahmed Ahvari ni fitnat khatam thai. Ahvari ye Dai no davo [claim] kidelo, magar te halaq thayo.

• Aap na Mazoon Syedna Ali bin Hanzala aap na moavin [ madadgar] tha.

• Aap ni Umr 100 varas ni hati. Aap 27 Shabanul Karim 612 H. vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr Yemen ma Ugmur [Haraz] ma chhe, magar jagah maaruf nathi.
