JUMADA-AL-AVWAL

03- Urus: Syedi Qazikhan bin Ali A.Q (Sidhpur)

3 Jamadil Ula - Syedi Qazikhan bin Ali a.q. (Sidhpur)
Mazoon-e-Dawat,

Syedi Qazikhan bin Ali a.q.
Vafat: 3 Jamadil Ula, 1141 H. 1728 A.D. Sidhpur

• Syedi Qazikhan bin Ali a.q. Doat-e-Badriyeen na zaman na Hudud kiram tha. Ilmul Aalim tha.

• Syedna Badruddin r.a. na aap Mukasir ane baad ma Mazoon tha.

• Syedna Nooruddin r.a. ni ibtedai [ shuruaat nu] taalim nu sharaf aap ne hasil thayu.

• Aap Sidhpur na vaali tha. Sidhpur ni aabadi ma aapp no hisso mohto chhe.

• Munafekin ye aap ne ghani takleef pochave che.

• Aap ni qabr dushmano ye khodi hati, magar aap na jhust mubarak sahi saalam nikla.

• Aap 3 Jamadil Ula, 1141 H. 1728 A.D. Sidhpur ma vafat thaya.

08- Urus: Syedi Mulla Wahedbhai Saheb bin Mulla Ibrahimji A.Q (Surat)

8 Jamadil Ula - Syedi Mulla Wahedbhai Saheb bin Mulla Ibrahimji a.q. (Surat)

Syedi Mulla Wahedbhai Saheb bin Mulla Ibrahimji a.q.
Vafat: 8 Jamadil Ula 1165 H. [1747 A.D.]-Surat
Zaman: 39ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Ibrahim Vajihuddin r.a.

• Syedi Mulla Wahedbhai Saheb bin Mulla Ibrahimji a.q. Ahmedabad na vatni hata, ane mohta vepari hata. Ahmedabad ma zulm na zaman ma Ahmedabad chhori Surat aavi ne vepar shuru kido. Char anna [25 paisa] ma be [2] lungi banavta. Chhoti [small] aamdani [income] ma ghar chalavta.

• Panche farz masjid ma parhta. Roz Surat na Aamil, Syedi Hamzabhai bin Syedi Qasimkhan a.q. ni hazarat ma hazir thaata, ane nani mohti khidmat bajavta.

• Shahrullah ma Mumineen ne jamarta. Aap na jaman ma hamesha barkat rehti. Ek rivayat ma chhe ke aap na pase ek barkatvalo charu [pot] hato, je ma kain pan muko, te koi waar bhi khute nahin.

• Surat ma jivare Dail Ajal Syedna Hebatullah Moaiyad Fiddin [r.a.] ne raj taraf si taklif pohnchi, ane aap ne chhupava nu thayu, te waqt Surat na Nagarseth Navlakhi Seth ye aap ne tehni haveli ma aasro aapvano inkar kido. Te waqt Mulla Wahed Bhaisaheb na ghar ma Weaving loom na niche aap moula ye aaram farmavelo. Navlakhi no inkar ehni barbadi no sabab bano, jivare Mulla Wahedsaab ni khidmat aap ne keva daraja par lai gai?

• Aap na Qubbat Surat ma Navsari Bazar na nazdeek chhe. Qubba Dail Ajal Syedna Taher Saifuddin [r.a.] ye 1339 H. ma banaya chhe.

• Aap na bairo Ratanbu [vafat-1100 H.] ane aap na farzand Mulla Jalal [Vafat 1177] ni qubur aap na Qubba ni nazdik ma chhe.

• Aap na Qubba ni bahar be qabr chhe:

Mulla Mohammedbhai bin Syedi Sultanali Rampuri [ vafat 1200 H.] ni qabar , je Bawa Mulla Khan Saheb ni nasal si chhe. Baju ma Miyansaheb Shk. Taiyebbhai bin Jafar bin Syedi Najamkhan najle Syedna Feerkhan Shujauddin Saheb [ vafat: 1107 H.] ni qabr chhe.

• Aagal Qabrastan ma jaata, Syedi Fakhruddin Shaheed [galiykot] ni nasal ma 7 mi pedhi na Shk. Nazarali bin Mulla Farhullah { 1299 H.] ni qabr chhe. ane haji agal, Shaheed, Syedi Abdurrasul Saheb [ Banswara] na bavaji saheb, Shaheed Nooruddin bin Mulla Alibhai ni qabr chhe.



10- Shahdat: Moulatena Fatema A.S

10 Jamadil Ula - Shahdat Moulatena Fatema A.S

Moulatena Fatema A.S
Shahdat : 10 Jamadil Ula 11 H. 632 A.D. Madina

• Moulatena Fatema a.s. na viladat ni tawalli karnar je char bairo hata, te hata Sarah ( nabi Ibrahim na bairo), , Mariam,

Aashiya (Firaun na javzah) , ane Kulsum ukhte Moosa Nabi a.s.

• Aap na juda juda naam .............

'Fatema' ['Chhoravnar'],

'Zehra' [Roshan Thavu],

'Batool' [Pak, aap ne Haiz nohtu aavtu].

Rasulullah s.a. aap ne 'Ummul Abiha' kehta, yaani potana bava ni maan.

• Rasulullah s.a. farmavta ke Jannt na logo ma chaar bairo afzal chhe: Khadija a.s. ( Aap na bairo), Fatema a.s. ( Aap na dikri), Mariam binte Imran (Eesa nabi na ma saheba) ane Aashiya ( Firaun na bairo).

• Moulatena Fatema a.s. ni shaadi moulana Ali a.s. na saath thai. Aap ni hayati ma Moulana Ali a.s. ye biji shaadi nathi kidi.

10 varas na sansar ma Moulatena Fatema a.s. ye koi din Fakr ke tangi ni shiqayat nathi kidi. Pote chakki pista, har kaam jate karta, Hasan Husain ni kafalat ghana shafaqat si karta. Aap no maqam kevo ke Farishta ghar ni jharu karta, chakki pista, ane Hasnain nu palnu jhulavta. Jibrail aap ni khidmat ma rehta.

• Fatema a.s. ne Rasulullah s.a. ye " Tasbihe Fatema " inayat kidi hati, je nu bayan asharah ma waaz ma aqa moula t.u.s. ye kidu hatu. Je tasbih apne roj farrizat ni namaz ma kariye chhe.

• Aap na farzando ma Hasan a.s., Husain a.s., Zainab a.s. ane Kulsum a.s. hata. Mohsin nu hamal to napak dushmano ye giravi didu hatu.

• Rasulullah s.a. na vafat na baad aap na upar dushmano ye ghana zulm kida chhe. Aap na shauhar Maulana Ali a.s. ne gala ma rassu nakhi aap na saame khenchi ne lai gaya. Aap ne tamacha mara, ane laat mari Mohsin nu hamal girayu. Aap raat din rota ane aap na aansu, Mumineen ni shaf'aat vaste jame karta.

• Rasulullah s.a. na vafat na baad 70 raat ma, aap 10 Jamadil Ula 11 H. 632 A.D. ma vafat thaya. Aap ne Jannatul Baqi ma dafan kida chhe.

• Dushmano ye aap ni qabr ni ghani behurmati kidi chhe. Qabr par shandar Roza mubarak hata te 1925 ma tori nakha. Ahle Bait na ane bija saghla Qubbat bhi tori nakha chhe.

11- Urus: Moulai Nooruddin A.Q (Dongaon)

11 Jamadil Ula - Moulai Nooruddin A.Q (Dongaon) Moulai Nooruddin A.Q

Vafat: 11 Jamadil Ula- Dongaon
Zaman: Imam Mustansir billah a.s. [ 427-487 H., 1036-1096 A.D.]

• Imam Mustansir billah a.s. na zaman ma Moulai Nooruddin [a.q.] ye Misr jai ne ilm hasil kidu chhe. Aap nu asal naam Roopnath hatu. Aap ni saathe Moulai Abdullah [jemnu naam Balamnath-][madfoon-khambat] bhi hata.

• 467 H. ma aap Gujarat ma aaya. Moulai Abdullah a.q. , ane Moulai Ahmed a.q. na aap saathi chhe. Aap ye Deccan ni taraf dawat ni tablig kidi. Deccan ma Paithan muqame qayam kido, ane vahan si Dawat nu Zoohur thayu. Be-2 varas aap ye Mumineen ne hidayat aapi.

• Aap ghana buland martaba na saheb chhe. Aap na sabab Deccan ma Iman nu noor phelayu chhe. Aap ni karamaat mashhoor chhe.

• Aap 15 Moharram Vafat thaya, urs 11 Jamadil Ula na din manavay chhe.

• Aap na mazar Aurangabad ( Maharashtra ) na najdik Dongam ma chhe. Haal ma vahan ghani taameer thai chhe.

• Doat Mutlaqeen ma Syedna Hebatullah Moayyed Fiddin r.a. 1182 H. ma yahan padhara chhe. 40 din aap ye yahan ziyarat kidi chhe. 1207 H. ma 42ma dai, Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. yahan padhara ane Qubba ni taamir farmavi. Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. varamvar yahan padhara chhe. Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) 5 vaar yahan padhara chhe.

Mazoon-e-Dawat-al-Haq Syedi Husain bhaisaeb Husamuddin
Viladat: 26 Jamadil Ukhra 1338h (Kaparwanj)
Vafat: 11 Jamadil ula 1438h (Mumbai)

• Syedi Mazoon saheb ne syedna taher saifuddin r.a.ye aa ayat inyat farmawi thi للذين احسنوا الحسنى والزيادة

• Ehna adad 1138 thai che

• Apni viladat kaparwanj ma thai che

• Apye 3 dai na asre maimoon ma khidmat kidi

• 18 zilhaj 1414 apne Mukasir na rutba 52 ma dai ataa kidaa

• 18 zilhaj 1435 53 ma dai apne Mazoon no rutba inayat farmaya.

• Aap 21 waras Mukasir na rutba ma rahya ane 3 waras Mazoon na rutba ma rahya ghani shan si khidmat kidi ane 102 waras ni umar ma aap wafat thaya che.

• 11 mi tarikh aap wafat thaya 1438 na saal ma.

15- Urus: Mawlai Dawood bin Qazi Ahmed A.Q (Ahmedabad)

15 Jamadil Ula - Mawlai Dawood bin Qazi Ahmed A.Q (Ahmedabad)

Moulai Dawood bin Qazi Ahmed Shaheed A.Q.
Shahadat : 15 Jamadil Ula 972 H. 1564 A.D.
Zaman: 24ma Dai, Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin R.A

• Moulai Dawood Marde Muttaqi tha. Allama Vali tha. Dawat ni khidmat ma janfeshani kidi.

• Aap Muzaffarshah III na zaman ma shaheed thaya chhe. Delhi ma te waqt Mughal Badsha Humayun hato.

• Aap na Qubba Ahmedabad ma Saraspur Area ma chhe. Agmedabad ni 'nav ziyarat' ma aa shamil chhe.

• Aap na qabr par takhti chhe, je ma aap nu nasab malum pare chhe. Aap Syedi Fakhruddin Shaheed r.a. ni nasal si chhe.

• Aap na Vaalid Saheb, Moulai Qazi Ahmed, Ahmedshah Badshah ye jivare Ahmedabad vasayu, te ma je chaar (4) responsible persons hata, te ma si ek hata. Aa char Sahebo hata: Ahmedshah Badshah, Moulai Adam a.q. Moulai Qazi Ahmed, ane Pir Ahmedshah.

• Aap na bhai Shk. Nizam ni qabr Ahmedabad ma Moulai Raj na Qabrastan ma Mouai Shamskhan na najdeek chhe. Ane aap na vaalid Moulai Qazi Ahmed ni qabr maoulai Raj na mazar si niche utri samne Gaje Shohoda ni moti chhatri si utartaj chhe. Qabr par takhti chhe.

• 1433 H. ni Ahmedabad ni safar ma Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) Moulai Dawood na complex ma Masjid ni iftetah vaste padhara hata.

17- Urus: Syedi Dawoodbhai Saheb Shahabuddin A.Q (Surat)

17 Jamadil Ula - Syedi Dawoodbhai Saheb Shahabuddin A.Q (Surat)

Mazoon-e-Dawat,
Syedi Dawoodbhai Saheb Shahabuddin A.Q
bin Syedi Ismail Badruddin a.q. najle Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin R.A
Vafat: 17 Jamadil Ula, 1336 H. 1918 A.D. Surat
Zaman: 51ma Dai, Syedna Taher Saifuddin R.A

• Syedi Dawoodbhai Saheb Shahabuddin a.q. aale Tarmal na nabira chhe.

• Aap 45ma Dai, Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin r.a. na pota chhe, ane Syedna Abdul Qadar Najmuddin r.a., ane Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin r.a. na bhatija chhe. Mukasir-e-Dawat Syedi Ishaqbhai Saheb Jamaluddin a.q. aap na shafeeq biradar chhe.

• Aap na Vaalid Syedi Ismail Badruddin a.q. , 50ma Dai, Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a. na Mazoon hata. Temna Vafat na baad Syedna ye Syedi Dawoodbhai saheb ne Mazoon na rutba ma qayam kida. ( 1326 H.) Te baad 51ma Dai, Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. na zaman ma bhi aap Mazoon na rutba ma hata ( 1333H.).

• Ikhlas ane Yaqeen na aap pahar tha. Taqwa ane riyazat ma alam tha. Koi waqt koi mumin ne sakhat [hard] kalemo nathi farmayo. Te saathe dushmano par ghana shadid [strict] tha.

• 47ma Dai, Syedna Abdul Qadar Najmuddin r.a. ni nas na shaahedo ma, aap ek shaahed hata.

• Aap jismani hakim bhi hata. Aap ye ghana nuskha banaya chhe.

• Syedna Abdullah Badruddin r.a. vafat thaya tivare aap ni umr ghani mohti hati, te saath sipara ni majlis ma padhara. Daizzaman Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. ne nihayat khushu si taslim bajavi sajdo dido. Dai ni hazrat ma aap hamesha sajdao bajavi hazir thata, jo ke te zaman ma kitnak logo ne sajda si inkar hato.

• Aap hamesha namaz ma Imam Moulana Zainul Abedin a.s. ni Arafah ni doa parhta.

• 1336 H. ma aap vafat thaya.

• Aap ni qabr Qubbat Izziyah na bahar chhatri ma chhe. Uper Phota ma Qubba Izziyah na pase frontmaj aap ni qabr [chhatri ma] dekhay chhe.

• Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin r.a. ni sange marmar ni chhatri, Aqa Moula T.U.S. na hukam si aap ni qabr mubarak par lagava ma aavi chhe.

21- Urus: Seth Chandabhai Karimbhai A.Q. (Mumbai)

21 Jamadil Ula - Seth Chandabhai Karimbhai A.Q. (Mumbai)

Seth Chandabhai Karimbhai a.q.
Vafat 21 Jamadil Ula, Mumbai

• Seth Chandabhai Karimbhai marde mumin deendar tha. Vepari hata. Haq na dhani par fida na thanar tha.

• Dail Ajal Syedna Taher Saifuddin [R.A.] na zaman ma Chandabhai Galla case thayo. Dushmano ye aap moula ne ghani taklif aapva chahyu. Te case ma Dail Mutlaq na martaba par humlo hato. Khuda Ta'ala ye aap ne kamyabi aapi, ane Dawat ni fateh mubin thai.

• 21 Jamadil Ula, Mumbai ma aap vafat thaya.

• Mumbai ma aap na mazar, Fort ma Badri Mahal na niche Masjid na sahan ma chhe.

23- Urus: Syedi Jafferjee Jivaji Saheb A.Q (Amreli)

23 Jamaadil Awwal - Syedi Jafferjee Jivaji Saheb A.Q (Amreli)

Syedi Jafferjee Jivaji Saheb A.Q

Vafaat : 23 Jamaadil Awwal. Amreli,

Zamaan Al Daail Ajal Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin ibne Syedna Zakiyuddin RA

• Syedi Jafferjee Jivaji's history dates back to 200 years.

• He was one of the Hudud Fozala during Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin's (RA) era.

• Incidences of Arwa-Khabisa are known to have been solved at Syedi Jafferjee Saheb's (QS) Roza Mubaraka.

• He was a hudud in the era of 42nd Dai, Al Daail Ajal, Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin ibne Syedna Zakiyuddin RA.

• His Urs Mubarak falls on 23rd Jamadi Ula.
