Shawwal ul Mukarram

03- Urus: Shehzadi Sakina Bhensaheba binte Syedna Taher Saifuddin AQ

3 Shawwal al-Mukarram - Shahzadi, Sakina Baisaheba a.q. (Mumbai)

Shahzadi, Sakina Baisaheba a.q.
Najlate Syedna Taher Saifuddin [r.a.]
Vafat: 3 Shawwal 1354 H. [1935 A.D.] Mumbai
Zaman: 51ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a.

• Syedna Taher Saifuddin [r.a.] na khubaj pyara Shahzadi, Sakina Baisaheba na valeda nu naam Fatema Aai Saheba hatu.

• Bachpan si aap zaheen, chaalak ane aap na aali nasab ni khubio ne shobhavnar hata.

• Majalis ma aap abyaat,ane tilavat farmavta to majlis ma vajad aavi jaatu. Aap no awaz ghano mitho hato.

• Shaban 1354 H. ma aap ne bulugiyat hasil thata Salgerah na din aap no Misaq levayo.

• Aap Be mahina maj, Shawwal ma faqt 24 kalak ni bimari ma aap wafat thaya. Aap ni umar 15 varas ni hati.

• Dail Ajal, Syedna Taher Saifuddin [r.a.] ne aap na wafat no gehro sadmo pohncho.

• Mumbai ma Charni Road station na samne aap na Mazar chhe. Roj hazaro mumineen aap ni qabr par hazir thay chhe.

04- Urus: Moulai Yusufji ane Moulai Taiyebji Shaheed a.q.

4 Shawwal al-Mukarram - Moulai Yusufji ane Moulai Taiyebji Shaheed a.q.

Moulai Yusufji ane Moulai Taiyebji Shaheed a.q.
Vafat: 4 Shawwal 1011 H. [1602 A.D.]Ahmedabad
Zaman: 27ma Dai, Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a.

• Moulai Yusufji ane Moulai Taiyebji Shaheed a.q. beve navjavan [young] Talibe Ilm [Students], potani shaadi ni raate navsa [dulha] na libas ma sabaq parhva vaste Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah [r.a.] taraf aavta hata, tivare dushmano ye rasta ma beve ne shaheed kida.

• Aap na qubba Rajpur Mohalla [Aaje Sarangpur] Gomtipur bridge ni pase chhe. Rajpur mohallo te waqt mumineen si aabad hato.

• Ahmedabad ni nav [9] ziyarat ma ni aa ek chhe. Qubat ni najdik Masjid haal maj Moula ye Hasanfir saheb si online iftitah kidi chhe. Mumineen ni rahaesh vaste Aqa Moula taraf si yahan flats bana chhe.

• Qubba ni najdeek ma ek bhonyru chhe, je ma, rivayat ma chhe ke, Dawat na kitabo na khazana chhupavela hata. Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a. yahan sabaq parhavata.

• Aap beve ni qabr par shaadi ni mannat levay chhe.

06-Urus: Syedna Hasan Badruddin bin Syedba Abdullah Fakhruddin AQ [17th Dai]

6 Shawwal al-Mukarram, 17th Dai-al-Mutlaq Syedna Hasan Badruddin RA (Yemen)

6 Shawwal al-Mukarram,
17th Dai-al-Mutlaq Syedna Hasan Badruddin RA (Yemen)

• Aale Valid na aap 11ma Dai chhe.

• Aap ye Husne Zamarmar ma tashreef raakhi Dawat na Elaam ne qayam kida. Mazoon e Ajal Abdul Muttalib Najmuddin[a.q.] ye aap ni khidmat bajavi. Syedi Abdul Muttalib ghana Shuja ane bahadur hata.Dushmano aap si larazta hata.

• 812 H. ma Syedi Abdul Muttalib aeez ma wafat thaya. Syedna ne ghano sadmo pohncho.Te baad aap ye Syedi Mohammed bin Idris Asaduddin [a.q.] ne Mazoon banaya.Aap ye ghana maal kharchi ne dawat ni khidmat kidi.Dawat na den ne adaa kidu. 821 H. ma Syedi Mohammed wafat thaya. Aap ye aap na bhai Syedna Ali [r.a.] ne Mazoon na rutba ma qayam kida ane mansoos banaya.

• Hind ma aa waqt Maulai Adam [a.q.] Vaali e Hind hata. Ahmedabad aap na zaman ma qayam thayu.

• Syedna Hasan ni sakha ghani hati. Talabatul Ilm na vaste aap ni khas nazar hati.

• 6 Shawwal 821 H. aap Zamarmar ma wafat thaya. Masjide Aqdam na sahan ma Doat Kiram na javar ma aap ne dafan kida.

07- Urus: Moulana Mohammed bin Taher, 'Harisi' A.Q (Yemen )

7 Shawwal al-Mukarram, Moulana Mohammed bin Taher, 'Harisi' A.Q (Yemen )

Mazoone Dawat,
Moulana Mohammed bin Taher, 'Harisi' A.Q
Vafat: 7 Shawwal 574 H. [1188 A.D.]-Sana'a-Yemen
Zaman: 3ja Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Hatim bin Syedna Ibrahim r.a.

• Moulana Mohammed bin Taher, A.Q Bija Dai, Syedna Ibrahim r.a. na Mukasir ane 3ja Dai Syedna Hatim r.a. na Mazoon tha. Syedna Ali bin Mohammed Valid r.a. ye aap si ilm hasil kidu.

• Aap fazle azeem na saheb tha. Taalwar ane qalam, beve na mahir [ expert ] tha. Har jang ma aap Dai ni saath rehta. Aap Sana'a ma muqeem tha. Aap sana'a ma wafat thaya che, magar aap ni qabr aaj maruf nathi.

• Aap " Baweesa na Saheb' si mashhoor chhe. , Doa-e-Aqle Awwal- [Baweesa ]- na likhnar maula aap chhe. Ilm-o-Hikmat ma aap mashhoor chhe.

• Aap ni mashhoor kitabo ma...

• 1-"Majmu-ul-Tarbiyah : Be bhag ma chhe. Ek ma Namaz roza na taweel ane Imamo par behas chhe. Bija ma Haqeekat ane akhlaak na bayan chhe.

• 2-"Risalatul Haqaaiq": Aamal, Hayula ane Aalamul Kabir na bayan chhe.

• 3-"Lamaul Anwar"- Ismail Nabi na bayan chhe.

• 4-"Al Hamimiyah" Syedna Hatim [r.a.] na dushmano ne jawab chhe.

• 5-6: Hadaeq-ul-albaab, Al anwar-ul lateefa, Haqeekat na kitab chhe.

• Aap 7 Shawwal 574 H. [1188 A.D.] Sana'a ma Vafat thaya chhe. Turbat ma'aroof nathi.

• Aap na baad Syedna Hatim r.a. ye Syedna Ali bin Mohammed Valid ne Mazoon ane Syedna Ali bin Syedna Hatim r.a. ne Mukasir na rutba ma qayam kida.

9 Shawwal-ul-Karim - Syedna Qasim Zainuddin [r.a.] bin Firkhan (Ahmedabad)

31ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Qasim Zainuddin [r.a.]
bin Firkhan
Vafat: 9 Shawwal-ul-Karim, 1054 H.1644 A.D.
Ahmedabad

• Syedna Qasim Zainuddin [r.a.] Moulai Abdullah Saheb (a.q.) [Khambat], ane Moulai Hasanfir Saheb [a.q.] *ni nasal ma si chhe.

• Yemen na Dail Ajal, 30ma Dai Syedna Ali Shamsuddin [r.a.] na aap Mazoon tha, ane Hind-Sind na Vaali tha. Syedna Ali (r.a.) ye aap par nas kidi, ane Dawat paachhi Hind aavi.

• Jumle panch (5) Doat ni aap ye khidmat kidi chhe.

• Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah [r.a.] na zaman ma Suleiman ni fitnat na waqt aap bhi qaid hata. Ej misal Ali ni fitnat ma bhi aap ye khidmat kidi.

• 9 Shawwal 1052 H, Ahmedabad ma aap vafat thaya.

• Aap ni qabr mubarak Qubbat Qutbiyah ma Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed (r.a.) na javar ma chhe.

10- Urus: Syedna Husain Husamuddin AQ [21st Dai]

10 Shawwal al-Mukarram - Urus

Babul Abvab, Dai-d-Doat,
Syedna Moayyed Shirazi r.a. bin Abi Imran
Vafat: 10 Shawwal, 470 H. 1078 A.D. Qahera

• Dai-d-Doat, Syedna Hebatullah bin Abi Imran Moosa al Moayed Shirazi r.a. ni viladat Shiraz (Iran) ma thai. Vahan aap na aaba ajdaad ye Fatemi Aimmat ni ghani khidmat kidi chhe. Yej misal aap bhi arsa tak vahan khidmat kidi.

• Shiraz na Badshah Abu Kalijaar ni fitnat na sabab aap ne watan muki devu paru. Aap vahan si Mashhadain Moazzamin [Karbala-Najaf] ni ziyarat vaste padhara ane vahan si aap Misr pohncha.

• Misr ma aap par ghana imtehanat thaya. Imam Mustansi Billah a.s. ni 40 din tak aap ne qadambosi naseeb na thai. Aap ye Imam a.s. ni hazarat ma Qasida mokla ane Imam a.s. ye javab ma aap ne ilm nashar karva ni khidmat sonpi.

• [Aap Moulana Ali a.s. ni zarih Mubarak par ghana valvala si arz kare chhe: Aba Hasane ya naziran Nazira......je apan Qurane Shareef ni tilavat na baad parhiye chhe.]

• Imam Zahir a.s. na taraf si Shiraz ane Ehvaz ma Dawat qayam kidi. Imam Zahir a.s. Misr ma vafat thaya, ane Imam Mustansir billah a.s. Imamat na arsh par mustavi thaya. 479 H. ma aap na hath par Imam a.s. ni Dawat Shiraz ane Faris na bija bilad ma qayam thai.

• Imam Mustansi Billah a.s. na farman mutabiq aap Misr ma Aale Mohammed s.a. na ulum nashar karta rahya. Je makan ma aap ni bethak hati, te "Darul Ilm" na naam si mashoor hatu, ane te Aljamea Alaqmar na samne hatu.

• Vahn Imam a.s. ni raza si har Khamis [Thursday] na din ilm-o-Hikmat ni ek Majlis aap parhta. Yeh misal aap ye 800 Majlis parhi chhe.

• Aap 'Babul Abvab' na azimushshan rutba ne pohncha.

• Aap talwar ane qalam beve na mahir tha. Aap ye qalam si Bagdad fateh kidu.

• Ibadat ma, rasmo rivaj ma, tijaraat ma, tamam jagah ye Maula ni barakat apne mile chhe. Mumin na maiyat ni Sadaqallah, Doat na urs ni Sadaqallah, Shaharullah na akhari Daska ni Doa, Idd ni Doa, Hafta [week] ni saat [7] Doa, Tasbihul Aazam ni Doa, Asharah Mubarakah ni majalis ni Arabi ibarat, Baweesa ni munajat, Khamis na Qasidah, ane roz parhiye chhe te "Aba Hasan' no Qasido............Aap na Ilm na khazana no koi paar nathi.

• Satar ni tamheed ma Imam a.s. ni raza si aap ye Syedna Lamak r.a.ne Ilm ma taiyar kida, je barakat Yemen ane pachhi Hind tak pohnchi.

• Aap Qahera ma 10 Shawwal ul Karim 470 H. ma vafat thaya. Imam Mustansir a.s. ye aap na janaza par Namaz parhavi, ane yej Darul Ilm ma aap ne dafan karva ma ayaa, magar aaj yeh makan ya qabr mubarak na asaar maujud nathi.

21ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Husain Husamuddin r.a.
najle Syedna Idris Imaduddin r.a.
Vafat 10 Shawwal 933 H. 1527 A.D. Masaar, Yemen
Mazoon-e-Dawat:
Syedna Ali Shamsuddin r.a.
Daur 15 varas

• Aale Valid na Syedna Husain Husamuddin r.a. 15ma Dai chhe, satar na 21ma dai.

• Husne Shibam ma aap qayaam farmavta.

• Aap na zaman ma Hind ma Jafar ni fitnat no aalam tho. Hazaro Mumineen shaheed thaya.

Jafar ni fitnat ma Mumineen na gharbaar lutaya. Ghana Mumineen iman par sabit na rahi saka.

Moulai Raj bin Moulai Hasan a.q., ni saye si ghana iman par qayam rahya. Dail Ajal Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) hamesha aap na bayan ma farmave chhe ke jo moulai Raj na hota to apan mumin na rehta.

• Moulai Raj bin Moulai Hasan a.q., Moulai Raj bin Dawood, Soni a.q. ane bija ghana Hudud Shaheed thaya.

• Akhar umr ma Syedna Husain ni tabiyat ghani nasaaz thai gai. Dawat na har umur Syedi Hasan bin Nooh Bharuchi a.q. lai uthta. Hind, Sind ane Yemen si je kagazo aavta te Syedi Hasan bin Nooh ni khidmat ma arz thaata.

• 10 Shawwal 933 H. aap Vafat thaya.

• Aap ni qabr mubarak Masaar ma chhe.

11ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Ibrahim [r.a.]
najle Syedna Husain [r.a]
Vafat: 10 Shawwal 728 H. 1328 A.D. Hisne Afeda, Yemen
aap no daur 42 yrs no rahyo.
Mazoon-e-Dawat:
Syedna Mohammad bin Syedi Hatim R.A

• Syedna Ibrahim [r.a.] aale valid na panchma [5th ] dai ane satar na doato ma 11ma chhe.

• Dekho Aap ni nasal aaliyah...Aap na vaalid [Father] Syedna Husain [r.a.] chhe. Aap na Jad [Dada] Syedna Ali bin Mohammed Valid [r.a.] chhe. Aap na bhai Ali bin Husain chhe. Aap na farzand Syedna Ali [r.a.] ane aap na bhatija Syedna Mohammed bin Hatim [r.a.] chhe.

• Shuru ma aap no qayam hisne afeda ma hato. later, aap Darul Unq'a ma rahyo je killo ane mahal secured hata. Aap yahan aap na rasala na saath rehta hata.

• Aap Ilm ane Fazal na Saheb tha. Dawat na nizam ne lai uthnar tha. Shariyat na faraez ane sanan ne qayam karva ma aap no khas maqaam hato.

• Aap na farzand Syedna Ali [r.a.] na haath par Husne Kokaban fateh thayu.

• Aap na zaman ma Muluk, Ashraf ane Zaidi na darmiyan ghani larai thai. Yemen ni zamin ma fasad no aalam hato. Aap ye Husne Afedah ne qayamgah banayu.

• Aap ni umr 92 varas ni hati. Aap Dawat na arsh par 42 varas rahya. Aap 10 Shawwal 728 H. wafat thaya. Aap ni qabr mubarak Hisne Afedah ma chhe. Aa pahar par charhvu ghanu dushvaar chhe.

• Dail Ajal Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) ye taaeed si 4 Doat ni qubur ni tehqeek farmavi, je ma ek qabr aap ni chhe.
