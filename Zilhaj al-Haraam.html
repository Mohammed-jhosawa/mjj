Zilhaj al-Haraam

01- Urus: Syedna Mohammed AQ bin Hatim bin Husain in Ali [12th Dai]

1 Zilhaj-ul-Haram - Syedna Mohammed r.a. (Yemen)

12ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Mohammed r.a.
bin Syedi Hatim bin Syedna Husain Valid r.a.
Vafat: 1 Zilhaj-ul-Haram 729 H. 1329 A.D. Hisne Afeda, Yemen

Daur-1 varas,1 mahino ane 21 din.

Mazoon-e-Daawat:
13ma dai, Syedna Ali bin Syedna Ibrahim r.a.

• Syedna Mohammed r.a. 'Aale Valid' na 6tha (sixth) Dai chhe, ane satar na 12ma dai chhe.

• 728 H. ma Dawat na arsh par ayaa. aap no Daur-1 varas,1 mahino ane 21 din rahyo.

• Fazal, zohad, ilm ane ilm ma aap kamaal ne pohncha tha. Yej misal fasahat ane balagat ma be-misal tha.

• Aap ye Syedna Ibrahim [r.a.] na zaman ma Haj kidi. Vahan ghana Islami oloma na saath aap ye ilmi guftagu kidi. Jivare aap pachha tashreef laya, tivare Syedna Ibrahim [r.a.] ye aap nu ajab shaan si isteqbal kidu hatu.

• Aap na kakera bhai Syedna Ali r.a. (13 ma Dai), aap na Mazoon tha ane aap ni husaam [sword] tha.

• Aap na teen shahzadao:

• {1} Syedi Ali a.q.--

• Mohta Shahzada-Aap Saheb-e-kamal tha. Ilm, amal ane taqwa ma be-misal tha. Syedi Ali a.q Hajj par aap na bavaji na saath hata. Syedi Ali a.q Syedna Mohammed r.a. ni hayaati maj vafat thai gaya. 729 H. Syedna Mohammed (r.a.) ye ehna gam ma dardnak Marasiyah likha chhe. Syedi Ali a.q. na be farzand, Syedna Abdullah r.

a. ( 16 ma Dai ) ane Syedi Husain Syedna Mohammed r.a. ni kafalat ma tarbiyat lidi.

• {2} Syedna Abdul Muttalib r.a. ( 14ma Dai), ane

• {3} Syedna Abbas r.a. ( 15ma Dai) hata.

• Syedna Mohammed [r.a.]. 1 Zilhaj-ul- Haram, 729 H. bimari ma vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr Hisne Afedah ma chhe.

04-Urus: Shk. Jafarji bin Shk. Abdulhusein Shajapuri (Ujjain)

4 Zilhaj al-Haraam, Shk. Jafarji bin Shk. Abdulhusein Shajapuri (Ujjain)

Shk. Jafarji bin Shk. Abdulhusein Shajapuri
Vafat: 4 Zilhaj 1240 H. Ujjain
Zaman: 45ma Dai, Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin r.a.

• Jalal Shk. Jafarji 43ma Dai, Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a. na hudud ma si tha.

• Aap na vaalid Shk. Abdulhusein bhi Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a. na hudud ma si tha. jemni qabr Shajapur ma maqbara ma chhe.

• Shk. Jafarji Ujjain ma vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr Ujjain ma Qubba Najmiyah na bahar sahan ma chhe. Bin Maula Hasan RA

06-Urus: Syedi Khoj bin Malak AQ

6 Zilhaj al-Haraam, Syedi Khoj bin Malek Saheb (a.q.) (Kapadvanj)

Syedi Khoj bin Malek Saheb (a.q.)
Wafat: 6 Zilhaj 1006 H. [1597A.D.]-Kapadvanj
Zaman: 27ma Dai, Dail Ajal, S. Dawood bin Qutubshah

• Syedi Khoj bin Malek Saheb (a.q.) no maqaam Dawat ma ghano uncho [ High ] chhe.

• Shuru ni taalim aap ye Dail Ajal Syedna Jalal Shamshuddin (r.a.) si lidi. Aap ye teen Doat, 25ma Dai, Syedna Jalal Shamshuddin RA, Dai 26ma Dai, Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah RA ane 27ma Dai, Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah RA ni ghani khidmat kidi chhe.

• Dushmano ye aap ne ghani taklif pohnchavi chhe. Dail Ajal Syedna Shekhadam Safiuddin (r.a.) ni saathe aap bhi qaid ma hata.

• Syedi Khoj bin Malek Saheb (a.q.) Vinayak ni nasal ma si chhe. Vinayak Gujarat na aalim Bahman hata je Arabo saathe vepar karta. Pehla Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Zoeb (r.a.) na zaman ma Vinayak Yemen ma potana qabila na saath Dawtul Haq ma shamil thaya. Aap na Paanch bhaio hata. Aap ye aap ni potani pehchan aap na kitab 'Bazarul Bidayat' ma aa misal aapi chhe: 'Khoj bin Malek bin Mohammed bin Ahmed bin Ibrahim bin Ishaq bin Vinayak'

• Syedi Khoj bin Malek Saheb (a.q.) na chha [6] Rasail mashhoor chhe...... 1-Sarvare Avliyah, 2-Nurul Huda 3-Bazarul Mohabbat va Sheefa, 4-Bazarul Bidayat, 5-Hadiqatul Janan, 6-Hadiqatun Naam

• Syedi Khoj bin Malek Saheb (a.q.) 1006 H. ma Kapadvanj ma wafat thaya. Aap na Roza aalishan chhe.

• Qabrastan ma ek masjid chhe, je ni Dail Ajal Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) 1430H. ma Kapadvanj padharela tivare, iftitah kidi hati.

13-Urus: Mawlai Feroz bin Ismail AQ

13 Zilhaj-ul Haram - Moulai Firoz bin Ismail a.q. (Ahmedabad)

Moulai Firoz bin Ismail a.q.
Vafat: 13 Zilhaj-ul Haram 945 H. ahmedabad
Zaman: 24ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a.

• 24ma Dai Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin [r.a.] na aap Hudud ma si tha.

• Aap ni zikar Syedi Khoj bin Malek saheb a.q. potana risala ma laya chhe.

• Ahmedabad ma Kankaria Railway Yard na najdik aap ni qabr chhe. Qabr par chhatri chhe.

• Aap ni qabr torva vaste Railway ye ghani koshish kidi hati, magar na tori saka.

• Dail Ajal Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) ahmedabad ni last safar ma nav ziyarat ma yahan bhi padhare chhe.

16- Urus: Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin bin Sulaiman AQ [24th Dai]

16 Zilhaj-ul-Haram - Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin [r.a.] (Yemen)

24ma Dai , Dail Ajal,
Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin [r.a.]
bin Suleiman
Vafat: 16 Zilhaj-ul-Haram, 974 H. [1566 A.D.] Taybah [Yemen]
Daur:28 yrs

Mazoon-e-Dawat: Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin r.a.
Mukasir-e-Dawat: Moulai Miyan Musaji a.q.

• Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin [r.a.] Yemen ma madfoon Hind na pehla Dai chhe. Sidhpur aap nu watan chhe.

• 23ma Dai Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin [r.a.] ye aap par nas kidi, ane nas no kagaz, Hind na chhela Vaali Syedi Qasim Taqiuddin bin Moulai Hasan [q.a.] par mokla. Syedi Qasim ye khud Sidhpur padhari Syedna Yusuf (r.a.) ne nas no kagaz pohnchayo, te waqt aap nadi par hata.

• Syedna Yusuf [r.a.] saat [7] bhaio ma, saghla si chhota tha. Aap na bhaio ma Moosanhaim, Malekshah, Miyan Jafar ane bija Yemen ma madfoon chhe. Bija Bhaio ma Shaikh Badan [ je Syedna Ajabshah r.a. na qubba na bahar chhatri ma madfoon chhe.] ane Miyanji Ahmdabad ma madfoon chhe. Aap na bavaji ye virasat no hisso aapo, tema si aap ye Haj no irado kidho. Aap na bavaji bhi aap na saathe thaya. Haj si farig thai beve sahebo Yemen padhara. Dai ni hazarat ma hazir thaya. Aap na bavaji Yemen ma Manakha gaam ma wafat thaya.

• Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin [r.a.] te baad Dai ni hazarat ma rahya, ane Syedi Hasan bin Nooh (q.a.) si ilm lhasil kidu. Te baad aap Hind padhara.

• Aap Dai thaya baad Sidhpur ma 5 varas rahya. Te darmiyan Sidhpur ma Mumineen ane gair qaum vachhe fasaad (riots) thayo, ane Mumineen ne ye saghla ye 'boycott' kida, Mumineen na saath vepar band kido, Pani band kidu, ane tareh tareh ni taklif pohnchavi.

• Aap ye Mumineen no ek navo mohallo gam na bahar aabad kido, navu bazaar banayu, je ma 24 dukano hati, ane Mumineen ne vahan sij kharidi karva Farmayu. Paani vaste ek kuvo khodayo, baju ma ek Masjid banavi, je aaj bhi Sidhpur ma maujud chhe.

• Te baad aap Yemen padhara. Vahan dushmano no jor ghano hato. Aap Zabid ma makhfi halat ma rahya. Aap na dushmano ye aap ne ghani taklif pohnchavi chhe. Aap ne Roman Badshah ye qaid kida. 7000 Ibrahimi ni zamanat par aap ne riha kida. Ye raqam Miyan Ahmed bin Ajabshah [Madfoon-Ahmedabad] ye potana vepar na maal si adaa kidi.

• Aap ye te baad Aqamatul Meqaab no quilo [fort] fateh kido. Aaj bhi vahan aap ni nishani maujud chhe.

• Te baad aap Ziyyah ma rahya. Masaar ma rahya, ane akhir ma Taybah padhara. Vahan si aap ye Hind ni taraf Syedna Jalal Shamshuddin [r.a.] par nas pohnchavi. Aap Taybah ma 16 Zilhaj 974 H. vafat thaya.

• Taybah ma aap na bhai Moosa Bhai Saheb ni qabr chhe. Vahan Syedi Nooh bin Bharuchi ni Masjid chhe. Ek vav bhi chhe. Taybah, Sana'a si 50 Km. duur chhe. Rasto ghano kharab chhe.

Mukasire Dawat,
Syedi Ishaqbhai Saheb Jamaluddin a.q.
bin Mazoon-e-Dawat, Syedi Ismail Badruddin a.q.
Vafat: 16 Zilhaj-ul-Haram, 1377 H. 1958 A.D. Mumbai
Zaman: 51ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a.

• Aap ni viladat 48ma Dai Syedna Husamuddin [r.a.] na zaman ma Surat ma thai [1305 H.].

• Aap na valeda Kulsumbai Saheba binte Miyansaheb Roshanali Burhanpurwala chhe. 45ma Dai, Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin [r.a.] na aap pota chhe.

• 1325 H. ma aap ni shaadi Syedna Badruddin [r.a.] na haath par Syedna Taher Saifuddin [r.a.] na saga bahen Asma Saheba najlate 49 ma dai Syedna Burhanuddin [r.a.] na saath thai.

• Asmabai saheba si aap ne farzand, Yusufbhai saheb, thaya, je navjavani ma wafat thaya. [1361 H.] jemni qabr Ujjain ma Rozat Najmiya na bahar chhatri ma chhe.

• Asmabai saheba na wafat na baad aap ye ehna bahen Zainabbai Saheba na saath kidi. [ 1334 H. ]Te baad Husainabai binte Mulla Hasanali Burhanpurwala na saath shaadi kidi.

• 1335 H. ma Syedna Taher Saifuddin [r.a.] ye aap ne Mukasir na rutba ma qayam kida.

• 42 varas tak aap ye Dawat ni khidmat kidi, je ma si 40 varas aap Surat na Aamil rahya.

• Aap ye chaar [4] waqt Karbala ni safar kidi.

• Nana mota sau aap ne khub chahta hata. Aap ne Mumineen ' Hamara Ishaqbhi Saheb' kehta.

• Masjid ni muvazebaat, Dawat ni khidmat, mumineen ni kherkhwahi ane ilm ni dosti, aap na ikhlak no majmo [sarvalo] chhe.

• 16 Zilhaj 1377 H. 72 varas ni umare Mumbai ma aap wafat thaya. Nariyalwadi ma aap madfoon chhe. Aap ni qabr par chhtri chhe. Hazaro mumineen roz aap ni ziyarat kare chhe. Front ma Mukasire Dawat Syedi Ishaqbhai Saheb ni Qabr chhe. javar ma Mukasire Dawat Syedi Salehbhai Saheb ni Qabr chhe.

27- Urus: Syedna Abdul Husain Husamuddin bin Syedna Tayyib Zainuddin AQ [48th Dai]

27 Zilhajul Haram - Urus

48ma Dai Dail Ajal,
Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin (r.a)
najle Syedna Taiyeb Zainuddin (r.a.)

Vafat: 27 Zilhajul Haram 1308 H. 1891 A.D. Ahmedabad
Age: 70 yrs

Maazoon-e-Daawat:
Syedi Ismail Badruddin

Mukasir-e-Daawat:
Syedna Mohammad Burhanuddin r.a

Daur: 6 varas

• Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin (r.a) ni viladat Ashura na din (1239 H.) thai.

• Syedi Jivanji [a.q.] na gharana na aap tisra Dai chhe. Aap ye chaar Doat ni khidmat kidi.

• Syedi Abdeali Imaduddin [a.q.] si aap ye ilm hasil kidu.

• 1267 H. ma aap ye 200 Mumineen na saath Hajj kidi, ane Karbala Shareef ni ziyarat kidi.

• Aap no aawaz ghano buland hato. Surat ni Masjid-e-Azam ma aap Waaz karta to mic bagair aap no aawaz Qubba tak sunato.

• Aap na zaman ma, 1272 H. [ 1857 A.D. ] ma English sarkar na samne Azadi ni ladai shuru thai.

• Karachi ma 1304H. 1887 A.D. ma aap ye Faize Husaini qayam kidi.

• Aap ye ghana bilado ma safar kidi chhe. Karachi, Lunavada, ane Khambat ni aap ni safar yadgar chhe. 1308 H. ma Aakhir ma Mumbai si Valsad, Pardi, Navsari, Bharuch ane Vadodra thai aap Ahmedabad padhara. Syedna Abdullah Badruddin [r.a.}, ane Syedi Abdeali Mohiyuddin [a.q.] aap na saath tha. Yahan aap bimar thaya, ane 70 varas ni umare, 27 Zilhaj 1308 H. aap Ahmedabad ma vafat thaya. Tajpur ni Masjid ma aap na janaza ni namaz thai.

• Aap ni qabr mubarak Mazare Qutbi ma chhe.

49ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin [r.a.]
najle Syedna Abdulqadar Najmuddin [r.a.]
Vafat: 27 Zilhaj-ul-Haram 1323 H. 1905 A.D. Surat- age 67 yrs.
Daur: 15 varas

• Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin [r.a.] ni viladat 1257 H. ma thai.

• Aap no zaman sadaat no hato. Takht-e-nasheen ni majlis Surat ma ghani shaan si thai hati. Aap na zaman ma Dawat nu lakho nu den adaa thayu.

• 1320 H. aap ye Karbala ane Hajj ni safar kidi.

• Aap ni mohta ma mohti hasanaat, Faiz shuru karva ni hati. Jeddah, Galiakot, Ahmedabad ane Jamnagar ma zaaereen ne jamarvani rasam aap ye shuru kidi.

• Aap na daur ma koi Abdulhusain ye Nagpur ma 'Mehdi Baugh' no firqo shuru kido.

• Nagpur maj koi Abdulqadar Chimthanwala ye potane 100ma Imam no dawo kari, 'Pidri Baug' no firqo shuru kido.

• Aap na zaman ni mashhoor tameerat :

Mumbai ma Market ni Masjid, Qutbi Masjid Madanpura Masjid; Kapadvanj, Dahod, Madras, Rangoon ane Singapore ni Masjid, Surat ma Musaraj ni Wadi , Sidhpur ma Musafarkhana, Madresa ane Shifakhana , Ahmedabad ma Faize Qutbi vagere.

• 27 Zilhaj 1323 H. aap Surat ma vafat thaya.

29- Urus: Ghanj Shuhada

29 Zilhaj al-Haraam, Ganje Shohada

Ganje Shohada- "Aseer-e-Ahmednagar"
Shahadat-1096 H. Urs: 29 Zilhajjatul Haram
Zaman: 35ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a.

• 35ma Dai Syedna Abdultaiyab Zakiuddin Saheb (r.a.) ane 36ma Dai Syedna Abdemoosa Kalimuddin Saheb (r.a.) na zaman Gujarat ane khassatan Ahmedabad na mumineen par ghani mehnat ane taklif nazil thai. Gujarat na suba ne Mogal Shaenshah Aurngzeb no hukam thayo ke Mumuneen ne qaid kari ne Ahmednagar lavvama aave.

• Ahmedabad, Gujarat ane Malwa na 900 mumineen ne suba ye qaid kari, chalta Ahmednagar taraf lai gayo. Saath ma Syedna Feerkhan (r.a.) na dilband Syedi Najamkhan ane bija hudud bhi tha.

• Lambo rasto, sakht garmi [scorching heat], bhukh [hunger] taras [thirst] ane soldiers na julm si kitna ye mumineen rasta ma wafat thaya.

• Baqi qaidiyo Ahmednagar ni jail ma wafat thaya. Bhingar gam ma ek Muslim Masjid ni pachhal ek khada ma total 30 mumineen ne dafan kida hata. Kafan ke gusal devani ni bhi tasdi lidi na hati. Kehvay chhe ke kitnak aseero no to jaan bhi haji baqi hato. Ehva begunah , bewatan aseero yahan madfoon chhe.

• Varso tak aa avvliyao ni qabr chhupi hati. Shohada ni wafat ni taarikh alag alag chhe, magar 27 Zilhajah na din quboor jaher thai, te si jumla shohada no urs te din manavvama aave chhe.

• 50ma Dai Syedna Abdullah Badruddin (r.a.) na zaman ma aa quboor par shandaar Roza banavvama aaya, ane 30 shohada chhe, ehvi takhti lagavi.

• "Adaeul Akhbar" ane "Risalatul Munira" ma Syedi Maulai Ajabshah bin Jalal na havala si kitnak shaheedo na naam lakhya chhe, te aa misal chhe: [ref: Nasime Sahar]
