08-Urus: Syedi Luqmaanji bin Mulla Habibullah (Surat)

8 Jamadil Ukhra - Urus Syedi Luqmaanji bin Mulla Habibullah (Surat)

Syedi Lukmanji bin Habibullah a.q.bin Mulla
Qazikhan
Vafat: 8 Jamadil Ukhra, 1173 H. 1760 A.D. surat
Zaman: 40ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Hebatullah Moayyed Fiddin r.a.

• Syedi Lukmanji bin Habibullah a.q. ni viladat Rampura ma 1099 H.ma thai hati.

• Ibtidai Taalim Syedi Khanji Feer Saheb a.q. si lidi. Te baad Syedi Abdulqadar Hakimuddin a.q. Saheb ye aap ni tarbiyat ane kafalat kidi.

• Aap Hafizul Quran tha, ane ghana ulum ma aap ne kamaal hasil hato. Haqaeq ane Fikeh ma aap mahir [ expert ] tha. Aap ye ghani kitabo na sahi [ correct ] nuskha likha chhe. Aap na haath na likhela kai kitabo Dawat na khajana ma aaj bhi maujud chhe. Aap ye teen Doat [ 38ma, 39ma ane 40ma dai ] ni khidmat kidi. Hamesha Masjid ma Namaz parhta. Pag ma aap ne taklif thai to Qibla ma rasi mukavi ne, te pakri ne aap Namaz adaa karta. Aap 'Dawood Roza' raakhta hata.

• Syedna Vajihuddin r.a. na zaman ma aap ye Haj no irado kido. Surat aavi gaya. Tamam taiyari thai chuki thi, samaan bhi bandhai gayo tho. Syedna Moayyed r.a. nu farman aayu ke Hajj par na jata. Aap rokai gaya, ane Dai na farman mujab Dawat na nizam ne sanbhali lido, jeni zikar Syedna Moayyed r.a. ye kidi chhe.

• 74 varas ni umare aap Surat ma vafat thaya. Syedna Moayyed r.a. ye aap na janaza par Namaz parhavi.

• Qubba Najmiyah ma Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. na baju ma aap madfoon chhe. Photo ma front qabr aap ni chhe.

14- Urus: Ganje Shohoda (Ahmedabad)

14 Jamadil Ukhra - Ganje Shohoda (Ahmedabad)

Ganje Shohoda a.q.
urs:14 Jamadil Ukhra, Ahmedabad
Zaman: 26ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah r.a.

• Ahmedabad ma Qutbi Mazar ma, Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah r.a. na Qubba si niche utarta , west ma ek chhatri ma Ganje Shohadah madfoon chhe. Te chhtri Ganje Shohada ni chhe.

• Gujarat na sultan Muzaffarshah III na Zaman ma Mumineen ne ghani taklif pohnchi chhe. Te na zulm-o-sitam ma aa Shohoda Shaheed thaya chhe.

• Delhi si aavi ne Mogalo ye aa Sultan Muzaffarshah III ne bhagayo, te waqt te kehto ke jo mein pachho Sultan banis to Qaum-e-Bawahir ne chakki ma pisi nakhis.

• Aakhare, khudqasi kari ne, te halaq thayo.

15- Urus: Syedna Dawood Burhanuddin AQ bin Qutub Shah [27th Dai] (Ahmedabad)

15 Jamadil Ukhra - Syedna Dawood Burhanuddin AQ bin Qutub Shah [27th Dai] (Ahmedabad)

27ma Dai, Dail Ajal,Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah Burhanuddin r.a.

Vafat: 15 Jamadil Ukhra, 1021 H. 1612 A.D. Ahmedabad

• Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah Burhanuddin r.a. ni viladat 23 Rabiul Awwal 945 H. Ahmedabad ma thai. Ma Saheba nu naam Gauribu binte Aliji chhe, je Hafizul Quran tha. Ghani chhoti umr ma Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah Burhanuddin r.a. ye bhi Quran-e-Majeed hifz kida hata.

• 21 varas ni umare aap ye Haj kidi, te baad Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. ni hazrat ma Yemen padhara, ane ilm hasil kidu.

• Aap na zaman ma Suleiman ni fitnat barpa thai. Suleiman ye aap na maqam no inkar kido, ane khud Dai no daavo kido. Haq na Dai ne ane Mumineen ne ghani taklif pohnchavi.

• Syedna Dawood char varas makhfi rahya. Aa waqt ma Syedna Shekhadam Safiuddin r.a. ane Syedi Alimohammad bin Firoz a.q. ane bija hudud kiram ye ghani janfeshani kidi.

• Khud Syedna Dawood Lahore, Akber Badshah na vahan padhara. Aap ni shanaat jaher thai, ane fateh mubin hasil thai. Suleiman Lahore maj halaq thayo.

• Te baad 15 varas aap Ahmedabad ma hata. Dushmnao ye te arsa ma bhi aap ne ghani taklif pohnchavi chhe. Aap ne qaid bhi kida.

• Aap 75 varas ni umare, Ahmedabad ma bimari ma vafat thaya. Aap na Qubba Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin t.u.s. ye banaya chhe.

• Aap na naam si Mumineen Dawoodi Bohra kehvay chhe.

• Aap ni baju ma aap na Aqeela Rani Aaisaheba ni qabr chhe, je Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a.(29ma Dai) ane Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed r.a. na valeda chhe.

• Aap na ek Shahzada Syedi Khanji Hakimuddin ane tena Javzah ni qabr Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a. na Qubbat na pachhal chhatri ma chhe.

15- Mawlai Ali bhai Shahid (Indore) (Kamlapur)

15 Jamadil Ukhra - Mawlai Ali bhai Shahid (Indore) (Kamlapur)

Syedi Alibhai Shaheed a.q.
bin Syedi Shamskhan
Vafat 15 Jamadil Ukhra, Kamlapur [near Indore]
Zaman: 34ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Ismail Badruddiin R.A

• Syedi Alibhai Shaheed a.q. Syedi Fakhruddin Shaheed r.a. ni nasal si chhe. Syedi Yusufji a.q. [madfoon- Shajapur] aap na biradar chhe.

• Aap Ujjain si Kamlapur Dawat vaste aavela. Aap na saath bija teen chaar sahebo Vajebat lai vapasi ma "Son Kutchh" gaam no qasad kido. Rasta ma lootaro [robbers] ye aap na maal asbaab luti lida, ane aap ne ane saghla sahebo ne Shaheed kida.

• Kamlapur na Mumineen ne khabar milta aap na jussa mubarakah ne Kamlapur ma dafan kida. Bija sahebo ne bhi yahan duuri par dafan kida.

• Aap Saheb-e-Mojezaat chhe. Ankho vaste aap ni mannat le chhe.

• Aap ni qabr par Qubba bana hua chhe.

• Kamlapur Indore ni najdeek chhe.

18- Urus: Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin AQ [42nd Dai] (Surat)

18 Jamadil Ukhra - Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin AQ [42nd Dai] (Surat)

42ma Dai, Dail Ajal.
Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a.
najle Dail Ajal Syedna Abdultaiyeb Zakiuddin r.a.
Vafat: 18 Jamadil Ukhra, 1213 H. 1796 A.D. Surat
Daur- 13 varas

• Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. Aale Bharmal na Satma Dai chhe.

• Aap ni viladat 23 Zilhaj 1177 H. ma thai.. Pehla aap Jamnagar ma hata.

• 1200 H. ma 23varas ni umare aap Dawat na Arsh par aaya, ane Burhanpur ek muddat tak rahya.

• 1209 H. ma aap Burhanpur si Surat padhara ane ehne 'Darul Hijrat' ikhteyar kidu. Dawat Hindustan aaya baad, 264 varas na baad Surat ne aa sharaf milu.

• Aap na zaman ma Majdu ye Ujjain ma ghani mohti fitnat kidi.Aap ni saaye si Dawat ni fateh thai, ane Dushmano zalil thaya.

• 1204 H. ma aap Jamnagar ane Ahmedabad padhara. Aurangzeb na zaman ma zalemeen ye Doat na Qubba giravi dida hata, ane qubur na asaar mitavvani koshish kidi hati. Aap ye Doat na Qubba banaya, ane Fozola kiram ni qubur banavi. Ej misal 1207 H. ma Dongam ma Moulai Nooruddin r.a. na Qubba banaya.

• Kathiavar ma femine ma aap ye ghanu fez jaari kidu.ej misal Surat ma sailab (flood) aayu. Te waqt bhi aap ye saghla ne anaaj pohnchayu.

• 36 varas ni nani umare aap Surat ma vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr mubarak Qubba Najmiyah, Surat ma chhe.

• Aap ye aap na biradar Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a. par nas farmavi.

18-Urus: Mawlai Adam ibne Dawood (Jamnagar)

18 Jamadil Ukhra - Urus Mawlai Adam ibne Dawood (Jamnagar)

Al moula,
Moulai Mulla Adam bin Moulai Mulla Dawood a.q.
Vafat: 18 Jamadil Ukhra, 980 H. Jamnagar
Zaman: 24ma Dai, Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a.

• Moulai Mulla Adam 34ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a. na jad (Grand Father) chhe.

• Aap na vaalid Mulla Dawood a.q. na baad, Vaali-e-Hind ye aap ne Morbi na umur sonpa. 24ma Dai, Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. no zaman hato.

• 944 H. ma Mogul Badshah, Humayun ye Gujarat par chadhai kidi, te waqt sultan Mehmoodshah vahan si bhagi ne Kathiawar taraf aayo, ane ghana gaam looti lida. Mumineen par bhi ghani musibat aavi pari. Morbi Kathiawar ma chhe. Logo gaam chhori ne hijrat kari gaya. Ghana muminaat izzat bachavva vaste kuva ma dubi gaya, je kuvo aaj bhi Morbi ma maujud chhe. Morbi ma Moulai Adam a.q. na makan ane kitabo bhi luti lida.

• Moulai Adam a.q. aam 960 H. ma Morbi chhori Jamnagar padhara, te waqt Jamnagar na vaali Moulai Feer Jamaluddin a.q. tha. Mulla Adam a.q. ye Jamnagar vasayu ane vahan 980 H. ma aap vafat thaya.

• Aap ni qabr Moulai Sheikh Feer a.q. na Qubba na qareeb bija Qubba ma chhe. Baju ma aap na Farzand Mulla Raj a.q. ni qabr chhe.

18-Urus: Moulai Burhanuddin Ibne Khoj Khan (Pisawada)

18 Jamadil Ukhra - Moulai Burhanuddin Ibne Khoj Khan (Pisawada)

Syedi Burhanuddin a.q.
urs 18 Jamadil Ukhra, Pisawara (Gujarat)
Zaman: 24ma Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a.

• Sitta Risael ma Syedi Khoj bin Malek saheb a.q. Syedi Burhanuddin a.q. ni zikar laya chhe.

• Aap marde muttaqi tha, aabid ane parhezgar tha.

• 24ma Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. ni aap ye ghani khidmat kidi chhe. Dawat vaste Pisawara, je Ahmedabad si qareeb chhe, vahan padhara hata, ane vahanj aap vafat thaya chhe.

• Aap ni qabr par Qubba bana hua chhe. Bahar mazar complex ma masjid bani hui chhe. Taamr nu kaam chale chhe.

• Gaam ma masjid aalishan chhe, jeno 1429 H. ma Hasanfeer ma Aqa moula ye net par iftetah kido hato.

• Pisawara ma Dawat na bija Hudud, Moulai Khoj Khan Saheb ane Moulai Ahmed bin Hasankhan Saheb ni qabaro chhe, magar maruuf nathi.

• Moulai Ahmed bin Hasankhan, Moulai Raj bin Moulai Hasan a.q. na zaman na hudud chhe, je zaman ma Jafar ni fitnat barpa hati.

23- Syedna Ismail Badruddin AQ bin Mawlai Raj [34th Dai] (Jamnagar)

23 Jamadil Ukhra - Syedna Ismail Badruddin AQ bin Mawlai Raj [34th Dai] (Jamnagar)

34ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a.
bin Moulai Mulla Raj a.q
Vafat: 23 Jamadil Ukhra, 1085 H. 1675 A.D. Jamnagar
Daur 20 varas, umar 95 yrs.

• Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a. Bharmal na aap Pehla Dai chhe.

• Aap ni viladat 990 H. ma thai. Aap na valid Mulla Raj Saheb a.q. ye Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a. ni hajrat ma aap ne Khumus ma arz kida. Syedna Dawood r.a. ye aap ne Ahmedabad na Daras ma dakhil kida.

• Aap Nagar na mohta shah vepari hata. Aap na valid na vafat na baad aap ye tamam karbhar sanbhalo. Char mahna aap nagar ma rehta, ane aath mahina Ahmedabad ma daras ma rehta.Te arsa ma aap sabilullah ma ghanu kharch karta.

• Aap farmavta "Agar Khuda ni rah ma kharchva si daulat khuti jati hoy to mara najdeek kai nathi. Agar Ilm hasil karva ma aaza ghasai jata hoy to mara ma taqat na hoti."

• Aap ye Saat Doat ni khidmat kidi. Panch Doat na zaman ma taqriban 40 varas tak aap Jamnagar na Vaali rahya. Aap par ghana imtehan gujra. Dawat nu ghanu dain aap ye adaa kidu.

• Talabatul Ilm par aap ni ghani shafaqat hati. Aap ye Talabat vaste safra ni rasam jaari kidi.

• Aap ye Imamuz Zaman ne manam ma dekha.

• 75 varas ni umr ma aap Dawat na arsh par mQAustavi thaya, ane 95 varas ni umare aap Jamnagar ma vafat thaya.

• Aap ni nasal ma 9 Doat thaya.

• Aap ni qabr Jamnagar ma Qubbat Badriyah ma chhe. Aap na javar ma aap na Aqeela Ummul Mumineen Budi Aaisaheba ni qabr chhe, je Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a. na valeda chhe. Tej misal aap na Aqeela Rumana Aaisaheba ane Gazala Aaisaheba ni qabr bhi aaj Qubbat ma chhe.

27- Urus: Syedna Lamak bin Maalik, Alhemadi RA (Lehab, Yemen)

27 Jamadil Ukhra - Syedna Lamak bin Maalik, Alhemadi r.a. (Lehab, Yemen)

Qazi ul Aalimul Jaleel, Qazi ul Quzaat,Syedna Lamak bin Maalik, Alhemadi r.a.Vafat: 27 Jamadil Ukhra 510 H.

1117 A.D. Lehab, Yemen

Zaman: Imam Imam Aamir A.S

• Syedna Lamak bin Maalik, Alhemadi r.a. nu watan Lehab thu. Imam Mehdi Billah a.s. na zuhur na pehle bhi Yeman na aa Ilaqa ma Mumineen vasta hata, ane taqeeyat ma rehta hata.

• Imam Mustansir Billah r.a. ye aap ne Yemen ma Qazi ul Quzaat na a'ala mansab ma qayam kida.

• Syedna Lamak r.a. ye teen Aimmat [ 18-19-20] ni khidmat nu sharaf hasil kidu. Aap ye Dawatul Haq ni khidmat ma ghani jafeshani kidi. Syedna Hatim bin Syedna Ibrahim r.a. ye aap ni khidmat ni zikar ghani tafseel [ details] si kidi chhe.

• Imam Mustansir Billah A.S na Zaman ma Yemen ma Hakim bin Abi Javhar ye Mumineen par ghana zulm kida. Mumineen ne qaid ma nakhi dida, ehma Syedna Lamak bin Maalik r.a. bhi hata. Syedna Ali bin Mohammed Sulehi r.a. ye Imam Mustansir Billah a.s. ni raza ane doa si Masar, Lehab, ane Hisne Hamzat ne fateh kida, ane Syedna Lamak r.a. ne qaid ma si chhuraya, ane aap ni qabeliyat ane jafeshani ni qadar kari, kitnak siyasi [ political] umur [tasks] aap ne sonpa.

• Syedna Ali r.a. ye Syedna Lamak r.a. ne Imam Mustansir a.s. ni hajrat ma mokla. Imam a.s. ye aap ne Syedna Moayyed Shirazi r.a. na ghar ma utaaro aapo, ane Syedna Moayyed r.a. si ilm parhva no amar framayo. Aap 5 saal Misr ma rahi ilm hasil karta rahya.

• Syedna Lamak r.a. ye Syedna Moayyed Shirazi r.a. na irshad mutabiq ulum na fuuz hasil kida. Yej misal Dawatul Haq ni rusum, Imami majlis ni aadaab, azmat ane safaf ne samjha. 5 saal na arsa ma aap ye je dekhu ane sunu, teno zakhiro [ collect] kido, je ulum na asrar [ secrets] hata, te haasil kida. Aap ye 27 sawal arz kida, to Syedna Moayyed Shirazi r.a ye farmayu ke aa sawaal na javab Imam a.s. si haasil kariye. Imam a.s. ye har sawal na javab aapa ane har javab ma Syedna Lamak r.a. ne ek qamis tashreef ma aapta.

• Aap na haath par Hind si aavela Moulai Abdullah a.q. ane Moulai Nooruddin a.q. taiyar thaya chhe.

• Aap ye imam a.s. ni raza si aap na shahzada Syedna Yahya r.a. ne aap na baad qayam kida.

• 20ma Imam ,Imam Aamir a.s. na Zaman ma aap vafat thaya. 510 H.

• Aap ni qabr mubarak Lehab ma chhe. Thora duur ek qadeem Masjid pase Syedna Yahya r.a. ni qabr chhe. Ziyarat karva lai jata nathi. • Dail Ajal Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) 1411 H. ma pehli vaar Lehab padhara,, tivare Syedna Lamak r.a. ni Ziyarat kidi chhe.

27- Syedna Qutubkhan Qutbuddin R.A (Ahmedabad)

27 Jamadil Ukhra - Syedna Qutubkhan Qutbuddin R.A (Ahmedabad)

32ma Dai, Dail AjalSyedna Qutubkhan Qutbuddin r.a.najle Dail Ajal Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a.Shahadat:
27 Jamadil Ukhra 1056 H. 1646 A.D. Ahmedabad

• Dail Ajal Syedna Qutubkhan Qutubuddin r.a. ni viladat 30 Zilqadul Haram 985 H. 1578 A.D. Ahmedabad ma thai.

• Aap Shahadat ni bisarat Rasulullah s.a. ye didi hati. tej misal aap na bavaji saheb Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a. ye aap ni Shahadat ni bisarat didi, te waqt aap ni umar ghani chhoti hati.

• 68 varas ni umare aap Dawat na arsh par mustavi thaya. Dawat qayam kidi. Elaam buland kida. Te waqt Ahmedabad ma Mumineen na 22 Mohalla abaad hata. Aa shanaat dekhi ne Dushmano ni aankh ma rai pisai gai.

• Dawat na Dushmano ane mukhalefeen- Qasim Andhlo, Abdul Gavi, Aurangzeb aa saghla laeeno ye mili ne aap na qatal ni tamheed ( planning) kidi.

• Dushmano aap ne qaid kari lai gaya. 6 gari bhari ne kitabo luti gaya. Kitabo ma si kai milu nahin.

• Khuda ni rah ma, Aale Mohammed ni Dawat ne hifazat karta hua, aap 71 varas ni umare, 27 Jamadil Ukhra 1056 H. Ahmedabad ma Karanj muqame Shaheed thai gaya. Dushmano ye aap na lasha mubarak ane matha mubarak ni ghani behurmati kidi.

• Aap na lasha mubarakah ne nadi kinare dafnavva ma aaya, je jaga aaj Panje Shaheed tarike olkhay chhe. Aap na mojeza zaher thaya. Mumineen aap na lasha mubarakah ne, Saraspur, Bibi na maqbara (Aaj nu Qutbi Mazar) lai gaya. ane vahan dafan kida. Voh mumineen je Saghriwala tarike mashhoor thaya, temni quboor M. Raj na qubba ni magrib ma aagal jaata aave chhe. Qabr par takhti chhe.

• Hijri 1373, Ahmedabad ma, Syedna Qutbuddin Shaheed (RA) na Urs ni Majlis ma Syedna Taher Saifuddin (RA) ane Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) ek Takht par jalwa numa thaya.

28 Jamadil Ukhra - Syedna Ahmed r.a. bin Mubarak a.q. (Yemen)

7ma Dai, Dail Ajal.
Syedna Ahmed r.a.
bin Mubarak a.q. bin Valid
Vafat: 28 Jamadil Ukhra 628 H. 1230 A.D. Hamdan, Yemen
Daur: 1 varas
Mazoon: Syedna Husain bin Syedna Ali
Mukasir: Syedna Al Qazil ajal Ahmad

• Syedna Ahmed r.a. Aale Valid na bija Dai chhe. Syedna Ali bin Moula Mohammed bin Valid r.a. na Kakaji na dikra chhe.

• Aap ni vasiyat mutabiq aap ne Hamdan ma Qubbat ma dafan kida, magar aaj te maruf nathi.

29- Urus: Syedna Mohammed Badruddin R.A (Surat)

29 Jamadil Ukhra - Syedna Mohammed Badruddin r.a. (Surat)

46ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Mohammed Badruddin r.a.
Najle Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a.Vafat:
29 Jamadil Ukhra 1256 H.1840 A.D. Surat,
age 30 varasDaur: 4 varas

• Syedna Mohammed Badruddin r.a. ni viladat 1226 H. ma Surat ma thai. Aap na valid Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a. ne ghani khushi thai. Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a. ye Shahzada vaste qasida likha; 'Kikabhai Jaldi Parho'.

• Aale Bharmal na aap 9ma, ane chhella [last] Dai chhe. 26 varas ni umare aap Dawat na arsh par biraj thaya .

• Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin r.a. vafat thaya, tivare aap ni umr 7 varas ni hati. 4 varas baad Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin r.a. vafat thaya. Aam aap ni kafalat Syedna Zainuddin r.a. ye kidi. Ibtedai ilm aap ye Syedi Abdeali Imaduddin a.q. pase lidu.

• 1837 ma Surat ma mohti aag ma Devdi mubarak ane Mumineen na ghana makano [10000] jali gaya. Te baad mohtu puur {flood} aayu. Aap ye Syedna Abdul Qadar Najmuddin r.a. ne Devdi ane Makano ni tameer nu farmayu.

• 1838 ma aap Poona padhara. Vahan Asharah ni Waaz ma Syedna Abdul Qadar Najmuddin r.a. par nas farmavi.

• Aap 30 varas ni nani umare, Surat ma Vafat thaya. Aap ni Qabr Mubarak Qubba Izziyah ma chhe.
