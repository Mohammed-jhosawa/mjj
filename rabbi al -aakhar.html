04- Milad: Moulana al Imam atTaiyeb Abul Qasim Ameerul Mumieen S.A (Qahera)

4 Rabiul Akhar - Milad Moulana al Imam atTaiyeb Abul Qasim Ameerul Mumieen s.a. (Qahera)

21ma Imam,
Moulana al Imam atTaiyeb Abul Qasim Ameerul Mumieen s.a.
Viladat: 4 Rabiul Akhar 524 H, 1132 A.D. Sunday- Qahera

• Moulana al Imam atTaiyeb Abul Qasim Ameerul Mumieen s.a. ni viladat, 4 Rabiul Akhar 524 H, 1132 A.D. Sunday- Qahera ma thai.

• Yemen ma Moultena Hurratul Maleka r.a. Imam Aamir [a.s.] na Hujjat tha.

• 524 H. ma Imam Aamir [a.s.] ye Moultena Hurratul Maleka r.a. ni taraf "SIJIL AL BISHARAAT" anepurana rumal mokla, ane potani Shahadat no ishaaro kido.

• 526 H. ma Moulana al Aamir s.a. na shahadat ni khabar Yemen pohnchi. Moultena Hurratul Maleka r.a. ye Jazira-e-Yemen ma Moulana al Imam atTaiyeb s.a. ni taraf si Dawat qayam kidi. Aap na naam mubarak si bay't lido. Yemen ni tamam masajid ma Imam Taiyab a.s. na nam mubarak si khutba parhaya.

• Misr ma Babul Abvab Moulana Ibne Madyan r.a. ye Moulana al Imam atTaiyeb s.a. ni taraf Dawat qayam kidi.

Abdul Majid ye qasr ane Zaheri mamlakat ni tawalli kidi. Ali bin al Afzal dushmani par qaem thayo. Misr par galbat karva no irado kido. Aimmat a.s. ni adavat zahir kidi. Abdul Majid ne qaid kari fitnat shuru kidi. Al Hasan bin Abdul Majid ye tene saath dido. Mumineen ne qatl kida. Amval luti lida. Babul Abvab Moulana Ibne Madyan r.a. ane Doat ma si char fozola-Naslan, Al Azizi, Raslan ane Qaves ne qatl kida. Fitnat aam thai, Zulmat chhaai gai, Moulana al Imam atTaiyeb s.a. satar ma padhari gaya. Magrib na bilad ni taraf sidhari gaya. Aap na saath Babul Abvab Moulana Abu Ali, hudud fozola ane mumineen kholosa mustatar [ parde] thai gaya. Misr ni haveli suni thai gai. Al Moezze Qahera na gharo khali thai gaya. Mumenaat chhaso [ 600] na qareeb magrib na bilad ni taraf chala gaya. Moulana Imam Al Amir khabar kari gaya hata ke aa misal zulmat vaqe' thase, fitnat qaem thase. Aap safar ni tamam taiyari kari chuka tha.

• Je waqt Ali bin Shahanshah Afzal si taaddi ane zulmrani ghani zaher thai, te waqt logo ehna taraf ushkeraya ane ehne qatl kari nakho. Abdul Majid ne qaid ma si bahar nikalo. Abdul Majid ye tena dikra Hasan ne qaid kido. Ek laakh logo Qahera na baab par aaya. Abdul Majid na saath mutalebat kido ke ehne dikra Hasan ne bahar nikale. Logo itna ushkraya hata ke qasr na darvaza jalavi dida. Abdul Majid ne thayu ke have aa logo manse nahi to teye Hasan ne zehr aapi mari nakho. Logo ehne maiyat na halat ma dekhi khamos thai gaya.

• Je waqt Abdul Majid vaste qaza saaf thai, amar sahal thayo, mumineen kholosa qatl thai chuka, te waqt ehno nifaq zaher thayo. Mamlakat ni lalach payda thai. Be [2] varas na baad 528 H. ma Imamat no da'avo kido.

• Moultena Hurratul Maleka r.a. ne potana taraf si khutbo parhva kahyu, Moultena Hurratul Maleka r.a. ye ehna upar hujjat qate' kidi ane dawat na logo ne khabar kidi ke Abdul Majid ye ahad ne tori nakho chhe, ane Imamat no jutho da'avo kido chhe.

• 531 H. ma aap ye Imam Aamir [a.s.] ni hazrat ma javaheraat na hadiya Sultanul ajal Ahmed bin Abil Hasain bin Ibrahm bin Mohammed Sulehi a.q. saathe Moulana al Imam atTaiyeb s.a. ni hajrat Aaliya ma moklaya.

• Imam [a.s.] na farman si Doat Muttaqeen na silsila ne jaari kida.

• 532 H. ma Moultena Hurratul Maleka r.a. 22mi Shaban 92 varas ni umr ma vafat thaya.

• Aap Dail Mutlaq Syedna Zoeb ne Dawat sonpta gaya. Aap na baad nas ba nas Doat Mutlaqeen no silslo jariya' chhe.

• Al Qahera ma Abdul Majeed ye zaaheri mamlakat na saath Imamat no da'avo kido, ane ehna baad daulat na vazra' ye ehna dikra Ismail Az Zafir ne 544 H. ma qaem kido. Daulat no saghlo karobar al Malek Saleh na hath ma hato. Jor ane zulm cho taraf phelayo, mumineen ni ginti kam thai gai, saghal mustatar thai gaya.

• Az Zafir thora varas rahyo, ehne qatal karvama aayo. Ehna baad teno dikro Eesa bin Ismail tifle sagir qaem thayo. Ehna baad ehna kaka no dikro Abdullah bin Yusuf bin Abdul Majeed qaem thayo, te 568 H. ma halak thayo.

• al Maliknnasir Yusuf bin Ayyub Misr ane Shaam ni mamlakat par galib thayo, je bani Ummaiya ma si chhe.

• Yemen ma Abdul Majeed ni Dawat muntalaq thai, Moulana al Imam atTaiyeb s.a ni Dawat baqiya' chhe.

• Aaj 53ma Dai, Dail Asr Syedna Aali Qadr Mufaddal Saifuddin TUS. si Dawat nu misr aabaad chhe Imam no qasr aabaad chhe. Ilm ane ataa' ni neel aap si jaari chhe.

08- Urus: Moulai Mulla Raj A.Q bin Moulai Mulla Adam A.Q (Jamnagar)

8 Rabiul Ukhra - Moulai Mulla Raj a.q. bin Moulai Mulla Adam a.q. (Jamnagar)

Almoula,

Moulai Mulla Raj a.q.

bin Moulai Mulla Adam a.q.

Vafat: 8 Rabiul Ukhra 1039 H. 1639 A.D. JamnagarZaman: 29ma Dai, Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a.

• Moulai Mulla Raj a.q. Aale bharmal na nabira chhe. Moulai Adam bin Moulai Raj na baad aap Hind na vaali tarike qayam thaya.

• Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a. na aap Valid Majid ( Father) chhe. Syedi Sheikh Feer Saheb a.q. si aap ye ilm hasil kidu.

• Aap Malekut tujjar tha. [very rich businessman]. Gujarat ane Kathiavar [ aaj nu saurashtra] ma aap ni ghani pedhio [offices] hati.

• Ilm-e-nujum [Astronomy] ma aap mahir [expert] tha. Roj kitab na 50safa [pages] likhta. Aap na khat [Handwriting] ghana saras hata. Aap ni taaweez ma asar ane shifa hati.

• Sultan Mehmoodshah na zaman ma Kathiawar lutayu, tema aap na 14gansdi [lot] kitab lutai gaya. Aap ne ghano sadmo [feel] thayo. Aap ye te saghla kitab fari si likha.

• Taalim [education] na zaman [ during his education period ] ma Moulai Raj a.q. bister [bed] rakhta nahin. Takiya [pillow] ni jagah iint [Brick] muki ne suta, jesi nind no galbo ( sleep) na thay. Raate aap uthi ne parhta.

• Har saal aap Dai ni hazrat ma vajebaat ada karva vaste Ahmedabad padharta. Aap ye aap na farzand Syedna Ismail Badruddin r.a. ne Syedna Dawood r.a. ni hazrat ma khumus [fifth son as per shariat] ma arz kida.

• Aap ni umr ghani mohti hati. Bimari ma aap Jamnagar ma vafat thaya. Aap ni qabr aap na valid Mulla Adam a.q. ni baju ma, chhatri ma chhe.

10- Urus: Moulai Abdur Rasul Shaheed A.Q (Banswara)

10 Rabiul Akhar - Moulai Abdur Rasul Shaheed a.q. (Banswara)

AlMoula,
Moulai Abdur Rasul Shaheed a.q.bin Mulla Noorbhai
Shahadat: 10 Rabiul Akhar, 1120 H. 1710 A.D. Banswara Zaman: 36ma Dai, Syedna Abdemoosa Kalimuddin r.a.

• Moulai Abdur Rasool ilm, amal, zohod, taqwa ane diyanat na saheb tha.

• 35ma Dai, Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a. [ Madfoon-Jamnagar] ni aap ye ghani khidmat kidi chhe. Aap Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a. ane Moulai Hasanji Badshah a.q. [Madfoon-Ujjain] na saath, Aurangabad, Dongam ane Burhanpur ni safar ma saathe hata. Aap vahan si Moulai Hasanji a.q. na saath Ahmedabad padhara. Ahmedabad ma Mumineen par ghano zulm no zaman hato, ehva waqt ma aap Mumineen ni dhaal bani ne rahya.

• 36ma Dai, Syedna Kalimuddin r.a. ye aap ne Vaghad ni taraf Dawat vaste mokla. Vahan Dawat vaste Galiyakot jata rasta ma 'Keral' gaon na paas ek Arab aap ni saath thaya. Aagal jata dushmano ye aap beve ne gheri lida, ane teer si jakhmi kida. Aap ane saudagar beve mara gaya. Dushmano ye aap na jism mubarak ne bhala par uncho kari shaheed kida.

• Aap mojeza na saheb chhe. Syedi Fakhruddin Shaheed r.a. ni misal aap na mazar par bedi tutva na mojeza mashoor chhe. Syedi Abdulqadar Hakimuddin Saheb a.q. ye, jivare aap Vaaghad ni Dawat vaste padhara hata, tivare aap ni ziyarat kidi chhe.

• Aap na bavaji saheb, Mulla Nooruddin bin Mulla Alibhai, qaidi na haal ma shaheed thaya chhe. Je ni qabr Surat ma, Mulla Wahedbhai Saheb na Maqbara ma chhe.

• Aap ni nasal ma Shk. Qutubbhai [Poona] Hafizul Quran tha, ane Dawat ni akhbar na aalam tha, je saheb ye ' Muntazeil Akhbar' kitab likhi chhe, ane je Mumbai na Aamil hata.

• Aap na Qubbat Rajasthan na Banswara ma chhe. Mazar na qareeb je kuvo [well] chhe, tena paani ma shifa chhe.

• Mazar ma haal developmnt ghanu thai rahyu chhe.

14- Urus: Syedi Ismailji Badruddin Shaheed A.Q(Godhra)

14 Rabiul Ukhra - Syedi Ismailji Badruddin Shaheed a.q.(Godhra)

Syedi Ismailji Badruddin Shaheed a.q.
najle Mukasire Dawat,Syedi Kalimuddin r.a.
Vafat:14 Rabiul Ukhra, 1200 H. [ 1786 A.D.]
Godhra- Gujarat, India
Zaman: 42ma Dai Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a.

• Aap aale bharmal na nabira chhe.

• Aap 39ma Dai, Syedna Ibrahim Vajihuddin r.a. na Navasa ( Daughter's Son), ane 41ma Dai Syedna Abdul Taiyeb Zakiuddin r.a. na Bhatija ( Nephew) ane Damad (Son-in-Law) chhe. Aap na bairo nu naam fatema bai chhe.

• Aap nu laqab Badruddin chhe.

• 42ma Dai Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. ye aap ne Godhra ni Dawat vaste mokla hata. Dahod jata rasta ma Saniara muqame Bhil logo ye aap ne shaheed kida.

• Godhra na ek Mumin ne khwab thayu, ane aap na lasha mubarakah ne Godhra lavi dafan kida.

• Aap na qubba Godhra ma Mohalla ni Masjid na sahan ma chhe. Development chalu chhe.

16- Urus: Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin R.A (Ahmedabad)

16 Rabiul Ukhra - Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin r.a. (Ahmedabad)

25ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin r.a.
bin Almoula Hasan a.q.
Vafat: 16 Rabiul Ukhra, 975 H. 1568 A.D. Ahmedabad
Maazoon-e-Daawat:
Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah r.a.

• Aap ye 23ma Dai Syedna Mohammed Izuddin r.a. na zaman ma Yemen hijrat kidi. Syedna Mohammed Izuddin r.a. vafat thaya, tivare aap Yemen maj hata. Mehnat na waqt ma aap ye Dawat ni ghani khidmat kidi chhe.

• Shuru ni talim aap ye Moulai Qasim Taqiuddin a.q. (Vaali-e-Hind) pase si lidi. Te baad Syedna Jalal r.a. ye Yemen ma Syedi Hasan bin Nooh a.q. na najdeek Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. ane Al Moula Malak bin Firoz na saath ilm haasil kidu.

• 24ma Dai, Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. jivare Hind si Yemen padhara, te arsa ma taqreeban 20 varas aap Hind na vaali rahya.

• Gujarat ma te waqt Mehmoodshah Sultan hato. Ghani fitnato thai, aap ne ghani takleef pohnchi chhe.

• Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin r.a. ye Yemen si aap ni taraf nas no kagaj moklo te waqt aap ghana bimar tha. Logo kahe ke bimar chhe to nas kahan pohnchse? magar Syedna Yusuf R.A ye farmayu ke nas ponanchsej , ane nas pohnchi.

• Aap faqt chaar (4) mahina Dawat na arsh par rahya. Aap ye beve Dawwod, Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah r.a. ane Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a. par nas farmavi.

• Aap ye Imamuz Zaman a.s. ne be (2) vaar khwab ma dekha chhe.

• 16 Rabiul Ukhra, 975 H. aap Ahmedabad ma vafat thaya chhe. Aap na qubba shareef Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) ye tameer kida chhe.

• Aap na qadam najdeek Moulai Shamun a.q. ni qabr chhe.

22- Urus: Syedna Musa Kalimuddin bin Syedna Abdul Tayyib Zakiyuddin AQ [36th Dai] (Jamnagar)

22 Rabiul Ukhra Urus

36ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
Syedna Moosa Kalimuddin r.a
najle Syedna Abdultaiyeb Zakiuddin r.a.
Vafat: 22 Rabiul Ukhra, 1110-1122 H. Jamnagar
Daur 12 varas

Mawazeen-e-Dawat:
1. Syedi Sheikh Adam Safiuddin a.q.
2. Syedna Noor Mohammad Nooruddin r.a.
Mukasir-e-Dawat:
Syedi Khanji Feer Saheb a.q.

• Ale Bharmal na aap teesra Dai chhe.

• Aap na Bavaji Saheb ni misal aap no zaman bhi mehnat ane taklif no hato. Aurangzeb ni huqumqt [reign] hati. Tena baad na Mogul Badshah, Bahadurshah na zaman ma musibato kam thai.

• Dushmano ye Jamnagar na raja Jam ne behkayo ke Syedna Kalimuddin r.a. na paase ghano khazano chhe. Jam ye teen lakh rupiya manga, ane Syedna Nooruddin r.a., Syedna Vajihuddin r.a. ane bija Hudud ne qaid kida. Syedna Kalimuddin r.a. ne aa sabab bimari aavi, weakness ghani aavi gai. Jam ne hundi lakhi aapi, saghla ne qaid si chhuraya.

• Aap ye Syedna Nooruddin r.a. par nas farmavi. 22 Rabiul Ukhra Nisfil Lail na waqt aap vafat thaya.

• Aap ni qabr Jamnagar ma Qubbat Badriyah ma chhe.

• Talebatul Ilm vaste aap ye kitab likhva vaste kagaz, qalam ane siyahi [ paper, pen and ink] aapva ni rasam jaari kidi.

Moulai Habibullah a.q.
bin Sheikh Sultanali Bharuchi,
Wafat: 22 Rabiul Ukhra, 1282 H. [1866 A.D.] Bharuch
Zaman: 47ma Dai, Syedna Abdul Qader Najmuddin r.a

• Aap Dail Ajal, Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. na nana chhe.

• Dawat vaste Bharuch padharela. ane yahan Bharuch ma vafat thaya chhe.

• Aap na javzah Sakinabai Saheba Sheikh Shahmalak [Burhanpur] ni nasal ma si chhe.

• Aap na qubbat Bharuch ma chhe. 1385 H. ma teni taamir thai chhe.

27- Urus: Syedna Dawood Bin Ajabshah Burhanuddin R.A (Ahmedabad)

27 Rabiul Ukhra - Syedna Dawood Bin Ajabshah Burhanuddin (r.a.) (Ahmedabad)

26ma Dai, Dail Ajal

Syedna Dawood Bin Ajabshah Burhanuddin (r.a.)

bin Firoz bin Nuh bin Jafar Assaajanut

Vafat: 27 Rabiul Ukhra , 999 H.1589 A.D. Ahmedabad



Maazoon-e-Daawat: Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah (r.a.)

• Syedna Dawood Bin Ajabshah Burhanuddin (r.a.) voh chaar sahebo ma si chhe, je saghla ye Yemen taraf hijrat kidi, ane Dawat ne ni taraf naqal karva ma janfesani batavi.

• Yemen ma aap Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin [r.a.] ni khidmat ma rahya. Te baad aap Hind tashreef laya.

• Aap no 24 varas no zaman, saadat no hato. Hind ma Mumineen ni 140 bilaad ma 10,000 si vadhare vasti hati.

• Ahmedabad maj Mumineen na 28 Mohalla, 22 Masjid, 1000 makan ane 200 Shops hati, ane Approx. 5000 Mumineen ni vasti hati. Ahmedabad Sarangpur ma Masjid aap ye banavi.

• Dawat na arsh par mustavi thaya baad, aap ye ghana bilad ma safar kidi. Deen na aalam ne baland kida. Saadat aam thai. Sindh na bilad ma Dawat qayam thai. Yemen ma bhi aap ne fateh azeem mili. Yemen ma je kitabo no ek mohto khazano lutai gayo hato, te vapas milo. "Hayya alaa khairul amal" ni nida zaher ma karva ma aavti. Idd-e-Gadeer-ul-Khum ni majlis thaati.

• Aap na zaman na shuru ma Gujarat na suba Mirjahan Kokeh ye ghano zulam kido. Aap makhfi thaya. Te baad suba Khan Khanan na hath par amaan milu.

• Aap na zaman ma Mohammed bin Taher Nehervali ni fitnat thai. Te ye Imam Mehdi hovano daavo kido. Fitnat na sabab aap ne Ahmedabad ma rehvu mushkil thayu. Aap Kapadvanj padhari gaya.

• 27 Rabiul Akhar, 999 H. 69 varas ni umare aap Ahmedabad ma vafat thaya.

• Kapadvanj ma Aap na be Shahzadi, Manek Baisaheba, ane Ajabbu Baisaheba ni Qabr chhe. Beve qabr paalkhi ma chhe. Qabrastan ma dakhal thata samnej qabaro na vachma aa palkhi chhe. vahan na mujavar aa quboor batavi sakse.

28- Urus: Kakaji Mulla Issabhai Saheb (Pratapgarh)

28 Rabiul Ukhra - Kakaji Mulla Issabhai Saheb (Pratapgarh)

Kakaji Mulla Issabhai Sahebbin Mulla Mohammedbhai Bin Bawa Mulla Khan Saheb a.q.
Vafat: 28 Rabiul Ukhra, Pratapgarh, Rajasthan
Zaman: 38ma Dai, Dail ajal, Syedna Ismail Badruddin (sani) r.a.

• Kakaji Mulla Issabhai Saheb ne Mumineen pyar si aap ne Kakaji Saheb si olkhe chhe, kem ke aap ne Syedi Abdul Qadar Hakimuddin Saheb hamesha kakaji kahi bolavta.

• Aap na Qubba Pratapgarh ma chhe. Mazar no complex ghano nafis chhe. development chale chhe.

• Moosaji Shaheed ni qabr gaam (where mumineen live) se thodi duur chhe, ye area nu naam housing board chhe, ye area ma koi mumineen nathi rehta. normally other caste naj che. aapni qabr upar qubba banela chhe.
